# 🌱 **1. The Five Instructor Principles**
These are the constitutional foundations of UMM teaching:

- **Reversible** — every activity can be undone  
- **Symbolic** — nothing is literal or emotional  
- **ND‑paced** — slow, predictable, gentle  
- **Non‑coercive** — participation is optional  
- **Non‑interpretive** — no meaning assigned to actions  

If you follow these, your session is safe.

---

# 🧭 **2. Instructor Compass (Your Pre‑Session Check)**
Before teaching, perform a quick Compass check:

- **North — Structure**  
  Know the session plan (even if tiny).

- **South — Safety**  
  Ensure the environment is calm and predictable.

- **East — Tone**  
  Set a gentle tone.

- **West — Cosmology**  
  Keep cosmology outside unless teaching it.

- **Center — Pacing**  
  Check your pacing and the group’s pacing.

This takes 30–45 seconds.

# ⭐ **7. Instructor Quick Session Template**
This is the **minimal viable UMM session** — 10 steps, all gentle:

1. **Start with Stillness**  
2. Compass check  
3. Draw one map  
4. Enter one world  
5. Meet one character  
6. Do one Play Tree action  
7. Group pacing check  
8. Add one soft detail  
9. Zen Reset  
10. Close the field softly  

If you follow this, your session is safe, constitutional, and ND‑friendly.

# 📘 **8. Instructor Troubleshooting (Quick Version)**

### **Overwhelm**
- shrink field  
- reduce to one symbol  
- pause  
- reset  

### **Drift**
- return to Compass  
- redraw boundaries  
- simplify map  

### **Pacing collapse**
- Zen Reset  
- remove symbols  
- slow transitions  

### **Individual needs space**
- allow quiet participation  
- avoid spotlighting  
- offer reversible actions
