# Style Guide  
### A Gentle, Bob-Ross-Style Approach to Writing and Structuring Your Project

## 1. Purpose
This style guide helps you write in a calm, clear, and friendly way.  
It keeps your documents gentle, non-coercive, and easy to understand.  
It supports pacing, clarity, and joy.

This guide is descriptive, not prescriptive.  
It offers suggestions, not rules.

---

## 2. Tone
Your project’s tone should feel:

- calm  
- friendly  
- steady  
- welcoming  
- patient  

The tone is the mood of the canvas.

---

## 3. Language
Use language that is:

- simple  
- clear  
- gentle  
- non-urgent  
- non-coercive  

Avoid pressure.  
Avoid complexity.  
Avoid overwhelm.

---

## 4. Structure
Keep structure light and breathable.

Guidelines:
- short sections  
- clear headers  
- gentle spacing  
- small paragraphs  
- simple lists  

Structure should feel like a well-organized palette.

---

## 5. Pacing
Respect pacing in your writing.

Suggestions:
- write slowly  
- stop early  
- pause often  
- avoid long, dense blocks  
- keep sentences friendly and light  

Pacing is part of the architecture.

---

## 6. Clarity
Clarity is a gift to your future self.

Tips:
- define terms  
- avoid jargon  
- explain relationships  
- keep sentences short  
- choose clarity over cleverness  

Clarity prevents collapse.

---

## 7. Non-Coercion
Your writing should never pressure the reader.

Avoid:
- commands  
- demands  
- urgency  
- forceful language  

Use:
- invitations  
- suggestions  
- gentle guidance  

Non-coercion keeps the project safe.

---

## 8. Small Strokes
Write in small strokes.

Examples:
- one idea per paragraph  
- one concept per section  
- one gentle point at a time  

Small strokes make big beauty.

---

## 9. Consistency
Consistency keeps the project coherent.

Maintain:
- similar tone  
- similar pacing  
- similar structure  
- similar terminology  

Consistency is the frame around the painting.

---

## 10. Stillness
Stillness is part of writing.

Pause:
- between sections  
- between ideas  
- between edits  

Stillness lets clarity rise.

---

## 11. Summary
This style guide helps you write gently, clearly, and joyfully.  
It keeps your project calm, stable, and easy to grow.
