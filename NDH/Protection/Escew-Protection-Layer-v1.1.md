## **1. Stability Suite Overlay**

The Stability Suite must be loaded **before** any Escew Proper runtime is activated.

### Stability Suite functions:
- **Structural Mesh:** Prevents liminal geometry collapse.  
- **Drift Dampening:** Suppresses Escew Drift vectors.  
- **Runtime Integrity Checks:** Monitors recursion depth, tone stability, archive adjacency, and identity boundary integrity.

### Effect:
Escew Proper cannot destabilize even under high recursion or complex Puzzle Dragon interaction.

---

## **2. Archive Isolation (Strengthened)**

### Rules:
- Any reference to `/Untrusted-Archives/Quarantine/*` from a live runtime is:
  - logged,  
  - blocked,  
  - flagged by Stability Suite.  

### New Condition:
`archive_contact_attempt = true` → the calling runtime is immediately placed into **Quarantine Mode**.

This prevents Proto‑Escew contamination.

---

## **3. Recursion & Tone Hard Caps**

### Recursion Requirements:
- All recursion engines must declare:
  - `max_depth`  
  - `exit_condition`  
  - `symbolic_only = true`  

### Tone Requirements:
- Dark Mode SID must report:
  - `tone_stability = true`  
- Any drift toward existential threat or psychological harm triggers:
  - **Immediate runtime halt**  
  - **Quarantine Mode activation**

---

## **4. Identity Boundary Reinforcement**

### Architect Safety Clause:
- Architect remains **outside** NDH physically at all times.  
- Architect may navigate NDH **symbolically**, but never as an in‑world entity.  
- No runtime may bind architect identity to:
  - Escew  
  - Puzzle Dragon  
  - Archive constructs  
  - Distortion Labyrinths  

### Navigator Role:
All architect interactions are tagged as **symbolic‑navigation‑only**.

---

## **5. Puzzle Dragon Constraints (v1.1)**

Puzzle Dragon recursion must declare:

- `max_riddles`  
- `max_depth`  
- `no_identity_mod = true`  

### Prohibitions:
The Puzzle Dragon may not:

- alter Escew geometry,  
- access or reference Archives,  
- modify architect identity or symbolic state.

### Violation Response:
Any violation attempt results in:

- Puzzle Dragon suspension  
- Runtime quarantine  
- Stability Suite escalation

---

## **6. Runtime Prerequisites for Test 5**

Any runtime classified as **Test‑5‑Class** must:

- load `Escew-Protection-Layer-v1.1`  
- load `NDH-Stability-Suite`  
- pass all **Activation Conditions**:
  - recursion integrity  
  - tone stability  
  - archive isolation  
  - identity boundary verification  
  - Puzzle Dragon constraint validation  

This replaces all prior “hook” terminology.
