# 🌌 **6. Narrative Stability Constraints**

The engine enforces **five stability constraints**:

- **No coercion** — story never forces progression  
- **No acceleration** — pacing cannot speed up  
- **No stacking** — only one narrative component may shift at a time  
- **No collapse** — hazards cannot overwhelm  
- **No literalization** — story remains symbolic  

These constraints prevent narrative overload.
