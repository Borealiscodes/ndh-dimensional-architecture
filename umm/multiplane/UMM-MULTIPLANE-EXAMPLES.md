# 🌱 **1. Map ↔ World Examples**  
These examples pair **navigation** with **environment**.

### **Example A — Valley Alignment**
1. Draw a small valley world.  
2. Draw a matching valley region on your map.  
3. Add a reversible boundary around the region.  
4. Pause and check Center.

### **Example B — Drift Synchronization**
1. Add a drift current in your world.  
2. Draw a drift line on your map that mirrors it.  
3. Place a tiny symbol at the start of both drifts.  
4. Close softly.

### **Example C — Region Echo**
1. Mark a shimmer region on your map.  
2. Add shimmer texture to your world’s sky.  
3. Add one soft detail.  
4. Zen Reset.
