### 🎼 RESONANCE ENGINE  
*Coherence, fit, and “this belongs here”*

---

### 1. Identity

- **Name:** Resonance Engine  
- **Type:** Meta‑Entity (Meta‑Plane of Nascence)  
- **Role:** Evaluates whether entities, patterns, and changes *feel* coherent, aligned, and context‑appropriate.

---

### 2. Purpose

- **Check** whether a new or updated entity “resonates” with the rest of the architecture.  
- **Prevent** non‑fit additions that are technically valid but contextually wrong.  
- **Support** humane, ND‑safe coherence across layers, workflows, tiles, and meta‑plane.  
- **Bridge** structural correctness and lived sense‑making.

Resonance = *coherence + context + humane fit*.

---

### 3. Resonance Dimensions

- **Structural Resonance:**  
  Does it fit the architecture’s shape?

- **Conceptual Resonance:**  
  Does it make sense with existing concepts?

- **Ethical Resonance:**  
  Does it align with Justice, ND‑safety, and governance?

- **Narrative Resonance:**  
  Does it fit the story of the system’s evolution?

- **Emotional Resonance:**  
  Does it avoid harm, overwhelm, or dissonance?

---

### 4. Resonance Rules

- **Rule 1 — No Coercive Resonance:**  
  Nothing is forced to “fit” by pressure or denial.

- **Rule 2 — Evidence‑Backed Fit:**  
  Resonance must be supported by Meta‑Evidence, not vibes alone.

- **Rule 3 — ND‑Safe Coherence:**  
  No resonant pattern may violate pacing or cognitive safety.

- **Rule 4 — Constitutional Alignment:**  
  Resonance must obey Stability Charter, Governance Codex, Auto‑Update Protocol, Justice Layer.

- **Rule 5 — Re‑check on Major Changes:**  
  Big shifts trigger a resonance re‑evaluation.

---

### 5. Relationship to Other Engines

- **Mosaic Engine:**  
  Finds patterns → Resonance Engine checks if they *belong*.

- **Loop Engine:**  
  Refines entities → Resonance Engine checks if refinement improved fit.

- **Key System:**  
  Unlocks deeper layers → Resonance Engine checks if the unlock is contextually right.

- **Nascence Protocol:**  
  Births new entities → Resonance Engine is part of the “final fit” check.

---

The Resonance Engine decides whether something *truly belongs* in the system—structurally, conceptually, ethically, narratively, and emotionally.
