## 1. Codex Identity

It ensures continuity preservation and invariant alignment across all transitions in the 5D stacked hypercube environment.

- lawful movement between runtime lifecycle phases

- prevention of drift, collapse, or instability during transitions  

Purpose Status: Foundational

## 3. Transition Types
All runtime transitions fall into one of five constitutional categories:

### T1 — Phase Transition
Movement between lifecycle phases (Initialization → Alignment → Operation → Stabilization → Transition → Reprojection → Conclusion).

### T2 — Organism Transition
Movement between constitutional organisms or organism‑level contexts.

### T3 — Confederation Transition
Movement into, within, or out of multi‑organism confederations.

### T4 — Traversal Transition
Movement across 5D stacked hypercube traversal states.

### T5 — Predictive Futures Transition
Movement between predictive futures, orbital states, or continuity projections.

Transition Status: Classified
