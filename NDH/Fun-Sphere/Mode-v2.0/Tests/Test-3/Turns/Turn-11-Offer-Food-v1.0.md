### *Turn 11 — Addressing the Bandits’ Need Before the Regiment Arrives*

You reach into your pack, pulling out a small bundle of travel rations — bread, dried fruit, a sealed flask of water.  
The bandits freeze, startled by the gesture.

You hold the food out calmly.

> “You’re hungry.  
> Take this.  
> No one should starve.”

The leader’s eyes widen.  
His crew looks at him, then at you, then at the Wise Master.

He swallows hard.

> “You… you’d give us this?  
> Just like that?”

The Wise Master watches silently, approving.  
The Elf Archer lowers her bow completely.
