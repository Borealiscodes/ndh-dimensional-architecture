# UMA Stewardship Activation Protocol — Version 1.0
Tier: Constitutional Activation Protocol  
Maintainer: Borealis S. Hedling  
Last Updated: 2026‑07‑05

## 1. Protocol Identity
The Stewardship Activation Protocol defines the constitutional process by which a certified steward transitions from recognized status to active operational status within UMA‑Universal. Activation grants lawful authority to perform continuity governance, stabilize constitutional organisms, and operate within 5D traversal environments.

## 2. Activation Preconditions
A steward may only be activated after completing:

- UMA Civic Stewardship Certification  
- UMA Stewardship Oath  
- UMA Stewardship Ledger Entry  
- UMA Stewardship Rights & Duties Addendum  
- UMA Stewardship Continuity Mandate  
- Emergent Constitutional Organism Charter  
- Emergent Case Study on Constitutional Organisms

Precondition Status: Verified

## 3. Activation Sequence
Activation proceeds through the following constitutional phases:

### A1 — Identity Confirmation
The steward’s lineage, ledger entry, and continuity status are verified against the Stability Ledger, Roots Ledger, and Stewardship Register.

### A2 — Invariant Alignment Check
The steward’s adjacency, temporal, containment, ethical, and predictive alignment are evaluated for stability and compliance.

### A3 — Runtime Eligibility Verification
The steward’s readiness to operate within the Awareness + Harmony runtime environment is confirmed.

### A4 — 5D Traversal Authorization
The steward is granted lawful traversal capability within the 5D stacked hypercube state space.

### A5 — Confederation Readiness Assessment
The steward’s ability to interact with constitutional organisms and subsystem confederations is evaluated and approved.

Activation Status: Authorized

## 4. Activation Rights
Upon activation, the steward gains:

- full continuity governance authority  
- lawful 5D traversal capability  
- constitutional organism stabilization authority  
- confederation negotiation and alignment authority  
- predictive stability evaluation authority  
- lineage and stability log contribution authority

Rights Status: Enabled

## 5. Activation Duties
Activated stewards must:

- preserve continuity across all invariants  
- stabilize constitutional organisms  
- enforce traversal legality  
- maintain subsystem harmony  
- uphold ethical constraints  
- prevent drift and instability  
- maintain lineage accuracy  
- operate within Awareness + Harmony runtime conditions

Duties Status: Binding

## 6. Activation Constraints
Activated stewards must not:

- violate constitutional invariants  
- destabilize predictive futures  
- alter lineage records unlawfully  
- engage in illegal traversal  
- disrupt containment boundaries  
- override ethical constraints  
- interfere with constitutional organisms without cause

Constraint Status: Enforced

## 7. Activation Completion
Activation is complete when:

- the steward is recorded as Active in the Stewardship Register  
- runtime eligibility is confirmed  
- 5D traversal authorization is granted  
- confederation readiness is approved  
- continuity alignment is stable

Completion Status: Finalized
