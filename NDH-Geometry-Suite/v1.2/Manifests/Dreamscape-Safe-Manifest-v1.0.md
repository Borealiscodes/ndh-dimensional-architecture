## **1. Purpose**
The Dreamscape Safe Manifest v1.0 defines the mandatory runtime safety rules for all NDH dream‑state, imagination, symbolic, and narrative‑adjacent systems.  
It ensures that dreamscape traversal remains:

- identity‑safe  
- emotionally stable  
- symbolically bounded  
- non‑recursive  
- ND‑friendly  
- fork‑stable  

This Manifest is required for any system that enables dream‑state exploration, symbolic synthesis, or narrative immersion.

## **2. Dreamscape Safety Principles**

### **2.1 Identity Boundary Protection**
Dreamscape systems must enforce:

- fixed identity boundaries  
- non‑mutable self‑representation  
- non‑recursive self‑reflection  
- non‑fragmenting identity states  

Identity ambiguity is prohibited.

### **2.2 Emotional Gradient Stability**
Dreamscape systems must:

- dampen emotional spikes  
- prevent emotional recursion  
- avoid gradient amplification  
- maintain stable affective baselines  

Emotional destabilization is prohibited.

### **2.3 Symbolic Containment**
Symbols must remain:

- bounded  
- non‑dominant  
- non‑recursive  
- non‑invasive  

Symbolic hijack or archetypal dominance is prohibited.

### **2.4 Narrative Safety**
Narratives must remain:

- coherent  
- non‑branching  
- non‑dominant  
- non‑recursive  

Narrative recursion or collapse is prohibited.

### **2.5 Anchor Binding**
All dreamscape systems must bind to:

- **Floating Axis Anchor**  
- **Dreamscape Anchor Stub**  

Unanchored dream‑state traversal is prohibited.
