# Glossary  
### Clear, Gentle Definitions for Your Project

## 1. Purpose
This glossary provides calm, friendly definitions for terms used throughout your project.  
It keeps language clear, reduces confusion, and supports stable communication.

Definitions are simple, gentle, and non-coercive.

---

## 2. Core Terms

### Term: Core
The foundational identity of the project — its heart, purpose, and principles.

### Term: Workflow
The gentle steps you take to build, edit, or expand the project.

### Term: Meta
The high-level map or aerial view of the project’s structure.

### Term: Boundary
A soft edge that protects pacing, clarity, and stability.

### Term: Stability
The quality that keeps the project coherent, predictable, and non-collapsing.

### Term: Appendix
Optional or extended materials that support or enrich the project.

---

## 3. Workflow Terms

### Term: Gap
A small missing piece — a definition, boundary, rule, or relationship that needs one gentle stroke.

### Term: Stroke
A tiny addition to the project — one line, one idea, one clarification.

### Term: Brush
The smallest tool you choose to fill a gap — a definition, clause, boundary, or relationship.

### Term: Stillness
A calm pause that allows clarity to rise and prevents collapse.

---

## 4. Boundary Terms

### Term: Non-Coercion
The principle that nothing in the project pressures or demands action.

### Term: No Urgency
The project avoids speed, rush, and pressure.  
Work happens at a gentle pace.

### Term: No Collapse
The project stays structured and breathable.  
Nothing becomes tangled or overwhelming.

---

## 5. Stability Terms

### Term: Coherence
The quality of fitting together smoothly, without contradiction or confusion.

### Term: Integration
The gentle way components relate and support each other.

### Term: Consistency
The practice of keeping tone, structure, and pacing aligned.

---

## 6. Documentation Terms

### Term: Template
A reusable structure for creating new documents.

### Term: Style Guide
A gentle set of suggestions for writing clearly and calmly.

### Term: Map
A high-level overview of how parts connect.

---

## 7. Summary
This glossary keeps your project’s language clear, friendly, and stable.  
It supports gentle growth and calm communication.
