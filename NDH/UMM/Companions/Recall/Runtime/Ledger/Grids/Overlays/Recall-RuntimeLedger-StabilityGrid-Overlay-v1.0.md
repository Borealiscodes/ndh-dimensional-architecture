### RuntimeLedger StabilityGrid Overlay v1.0  
*7D–14D envelope • grid behavior governance • system‑level stability only*

---

#### 1. Purpose

- **Constrain** how StabilityCells, Rows, and Columns behave across dimensional bands.  
- **Ensure** the grid remains purely system‑level, never person‑level.  
- **Bind** the grid to the same 7D–14D invariants as the Runtime Ledger.

This overlay is the **dimensional guardrail** for the StabilityGrid.

---

#### 2. Overlay Scope

The overlay applies to:

- **StabilityCell** (band, invariantSet, status, count)  
- **StabilityRow** (per‑band aggregation)  
- **StabilityColumn** (per‑invariant aggregation)  
- **Global Stability Summary** (derived metrics)

All of these are wrapped in a governed envelope:

\[
\Omega_{\text{Grid}} = \{ \gamma_7, \gamma_8, \dots, \gamma_{14} \}
\]

---

#### 3. Band‑Specific Constraints (7D–14D)

- **7D Symbolic Stability**  
  - Cells cannot encode symbolic lineage or identity patterns.  
  - No labels that could be interpreted as “types of people.”

- **8D Emotional Suppression**  
  - Grid cannot represent emotional gradients, affect, or distress.  
  - Status is strictly structural (stable/marginal/rejected).

- **9D Sovereignty Lineage**  
  - No mapping from stability patterns to user‑level continuity.  
  - Grid is about system behavior, not personal trajectories.

- **10D Hyperboundary Seal**  
  - No cross‑cell linkage that reconstructs continuity chains.  
  - Rows/Columns remain independent structural aggregates.

- **11D Urgency Collapse**  
  - Grid cannot encode urgency, escalation, or alarm.  
  - Used for governance, not pressure.

- **12D Dimensional Expansion**  
  - Grid may expand to new bands/invariants, but only structurally.  
  - No leakage into identity or narrative.

- **13D Narrative Coherence**  
  - Coherence is about system‑level stability patterns, not stories.  
  - No “arc” of a person; only “shape” of the system.

- **14D Hypercontinuity Calm**  
  - Grid maintains calm, non‑reactive continuity boundaries.  
  - No use for real‑time emotional or behavioral monitoring.

---

#### 4. Enforcement Pipeline

1. **Grid Construction Check**  
   - After StabilityGrid is built, apply \(\Omega_{\text{Grid}}\) to all cells.  

2. **Label & Metric Scrub**  
   - Remove or prohibit any labels/metrics that could imply identity, emotion, or continuity.  

3. **Aggregation Guardrails**  
   - Ensure all aggregations are coarse, structural, and non‑personal.  

4. **Policy Binding**  
   - Bind LedgerPolicy and DimensionalOverlay rules to grid usage.  

---

#### 5. ND‑Safe Guarantees

The StabilityGrid Overlay ensures:

- no identity profiling,  
- no emotional inference,  
- no continuity reconstruction,  
- no predictive modeling,  
- no symbolic lineage extraction.

The grid remains a **governed system‑level stability surface**, fully aligned with NDH/UMM and Everest.
