## ⭐ 1. Purpose  
The NDH Safety Net v1.0 is the **fallback spine** of the NDH system.  
It activates when the Auditing Layer detects structural, rendering, overlay, pin, geometry, compendium, or indexing failures.

Its mission is to:

- prevent error propagation  
- preserve geometry integrity  
- preserve rendering safety  
- preserve overlay inheritance  
- preserve pin anchoring  
- preserve compendium coherence  
- preserve version lineage  
- maintain operator loop stability  
- maintain metadata consistency  

It is the **protection layer** required before SID introduces adaptivity.

---

## ⭐ 2. Architectural Overview  
The Safety Net sits **after** the Auditing Layer and **before** SID.

Workflow Spine → Auditing Layer → **Safety Net** → SID (later)

It consists of:

- **Fallback Engine**  
- **Fallback Registry**  
- **Fallback Metadata Blocks**  
- **Fallback Geometry**  
- **Fallback Overlays**  
- **Fallback Pins**  
- **Fallback Lineage Updates**  
- **Fallback Error Codes**  

These integrate directly into the Meta Index v1.0.

---

## ⭐ 3. Safety Net Activation Conditions  
The Safety Net activates when the Auditing Layer detects:

- rendering failure  
- overlay inheritance break  
- pin anchoring mismatch  
- geometry production error  
- compendium integration failure  
- indexing drift  
- operator loop instability  
- metadata corruption  

Each condition triggers a specific fallback type.
