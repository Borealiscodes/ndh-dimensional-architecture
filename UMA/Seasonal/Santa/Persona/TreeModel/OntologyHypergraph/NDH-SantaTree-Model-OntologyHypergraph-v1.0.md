## **I. Hypergraph Overview**

A **hypergraph** differs from a graph in one critical way:

- **Edges may connect multiple nodes simultaneously**, not just pairs.

The Santa Tree Model requires a hypergraph because:

- seasonal meaning is multi‑node  
- ritual actions bind across layers  
- dimensional bands operate in clusters  
- persona runtime binds to entire node sets
