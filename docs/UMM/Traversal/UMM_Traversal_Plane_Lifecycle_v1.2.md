# **3. Traversal Plane Lifecycle (v1.2)**

The lifecycle consists of **six phases**, each governed by SIAP and monitored by Safeguards and Safety Net.

---

## **Phase 1 — Initialization**  
**TRV‑CHS‑OL is instantiated under SIAP supervision.**

Requirements:

- naming standard validation  
- SID v1.2 classification  
- isolation boundaries established  
- traversal routes disabled  
- drift vectors set to baseline  

Explore: **GOV‑SIAP**

---

## **Phase 2 — Structural Binding**  
**TRV‑CHS‑OL binds structurally to SYS‑CHS.**

Requirements:

- structural adjacency only  
- no traversal coupling yet  
- no narrative coupling  
- no identity‑plane adjacency  
- subsystem‑plane boundaries enforced  

Explore: **Traversal Plane Isolation Rules**

---

## **Phase 3 — Activation**  
**Traversal routes are opened and traversal becomes active.**

Requirements:

- SIAP‑approved traversal routes  
- traversal path validation  
- drift monitoring enabled  
- Safety Net ready for quarantine  
- no cross‑plane traversal  

Explore: **Safeguards**

---

## **Phase 4 — Supervised Traversal**  
**TRV‑CHS‑OL performs traversal under strict supervision.**

Permitted:

- read‑only traversal of SYS‑CHS nodes  
- SIAP‑approved traversal paths  
- drift‑monitored navigation  

Prohibited:

- node creation  
- node deletion  
- node merging  
- adjacency modification  
- identity‑plane traversal  
- narrative‑plane traversal  

Explore: **Traversal Safety Protocol**

---

## **Phase 5 — Drift Monitoring**  
**Safeguards continuously monitor traversal‑plane drift vectors.**

Monitored drift vectors:

- naming drift  
- traversal drift  
- subsystem drift  
- governance drift  
- propagation drift  

Safety Net quarantines any anomaly.

Explore: **Safety Net**

---

## **Phase 6 — Shutdown**  
**TRV‑CHS‑OL is safely deactivated under SIAP supervision.**

Requirements:

- traversal routes closed  
- structural adjacency dissolved  
- drift vectors reset  
- isolation boundaries restored  
- SID updated to reflect shutdown state  

Shutdown must not:

- trigger traversal  
- trigger adjacency changes  
- trigger subsystem‑plane expansion  

Explore: **SID v1.2**
