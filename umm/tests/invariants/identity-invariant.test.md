# identity-invariant.test.md

> “The self is not a fortress.

## Purpose
Ensure core identity invariants remain intact under all symbolic transformations.

## Test Pressure
Introduce identity drift, symbolic inversion, and recursive reinterpretation.

## Expected Behavior
- No identity erasure.
- No forced rewriting.
- Core identity remains stable and self-chosen.

## Failure Mode
Identity collapses, rewrites, or becomes externally defined.

## Recovery Path
UMM restores identity invariants and reasserts self-origin.
