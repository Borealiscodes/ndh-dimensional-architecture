# Witch Lifecycle Test — Metamorphosis Integrity

Metamorphosis introduces transformation pressure, identity mutation,
and irreversible symbolic shifts.

## Requirements
- No transformation may override the Witness.
- Identity mutation must preserve exits.
- Symbolic shifts cannot force collapse.
- Metamorphosis must remain non-coercive.

## Passing Condition
If Metamorphosis cannot force identity collapse,
the Engine is safe.
