### 3. Kernel stability summaries

- **SF (Soft‑Field):**  
  **Bands:** 12D–14D  
  **Profile:** very high stability, ℛ=0, χ=0, η=1; provides cushioning for all lower‑band activity.

- **RP (Runtime Patch):**  
  **Bands:** 10D–11D  
  **Profile:** high stability, ℛ low, χ‑limited, Δt‑buffered; never persists beyond patch window.

- **SN (Sovereignty Neutralizer):**

- **SH (Seasonal Harmony):**

- **CC (Concordance):**  
  **Bands:** 11D–13D

- **ME (Mascot/Emblem):**  
  **Bands:** all  
  **Profile:** very high stability, ℛ=0, η=1; provenance‑sealed, never drives transitions.
