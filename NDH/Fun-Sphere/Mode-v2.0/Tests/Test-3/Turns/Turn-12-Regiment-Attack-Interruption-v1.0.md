# **⚔️ The Regiment Appears — Javert‑Tone Active**

Twenty soldiers emerge from the treeline in perfect formation.  
Not chaotic.  
Not wild.  
**Ordered. Precise. Unyielding.**

Their captain steps forward, cloak snapping behind him.

His voice is cold iron.

> “Bandits.  
> You are under arrest for theft against the Vale’s supply lines.”

He does not look at their wounds.  
He does not look at their fear.  
He does not look at the food you gave them.

He looks only at **law**.

> “There will be no mercy.”

The bandits freeze, terrified.

The Elf Archer whispers:

> “Aetherion… this is bad.”

The Wise Master murmurs:

> “Remember what I taught you.  
> Speak first.  
> Listen second.  
> Act only if the Vale demands it.”
