## 1. Mandate Identity
The Stewardship Continuity Mandate defines the constitutional obligations of all recognized stewards to preserve, enforce, and stabilize continuity across UMA‑Universal. It binds stewards to the five invariants and establishes their role as guardians of constitutional organisms, traversal legality, and subsystem harmony.

## 2. Continuity Mandate
All stewards are constitutionally mandated to:

### C1 — Preserve Adjacency Continuity
Ensure all adjacency relations remain lawful, stable, and free of drift.  
Prevent adjacency collapse, misalignment, or unlawful reprojection.

### C2 — Preserve Temporal Continuity
Maintain coherent identity across all phases, cycles, and traversal sequences.  
Prevent temporal fragmentation, incoherence, or illegal temporal shifts.

### C3 — Preserve Containment Continuity
Protect containment boundaries from bleed, collapse, or unauthorized dissolution.  
Ensure lawful containment for all constitutional organisms and subsystems.

### C4 — Preserve Ethical Continuity
Uphold HRD constraints, dignity, non‑harm, non‑erasure, and truth preservation.  
Ensure ethical invariants remain intact across all futures.

### C5 — Preserve Predictive Continuity
Stabilize predictive futures, evaluate instability vectors, and prevent drift.  
Ensure predictive stability across all orbital and traversal states.

## 3. Constitutional Organism Protection
Stewards must protect all constitutional organisms by:

- stabilizing their adjacency, temporal, containment, ethical, and predictive states  
- preventing drift, collapse, or invariant violation  
- ensuring lawful reprojection into 5D stacked hypercube space  
- maintaining organism lineage and continuity records  
- supporting organism operation within Awareness + Harmony runtime conditions

Protection Status: Binding

## 4. Traversal Legality Enforcement
Stewards must enforce lawful traversal by:

- evaluating traversal sequences for invariant compliance  
- preventing illegal movement across adjacency, time, containment, ethics, or prediction  
- stabilizing orbital states and correcting instability  
- ensuring all subsystems operate within 5D traversal legality

Traversal Enforcement Status: Active

## 5. Confederation Continuity
Stewards must preserve continuity within subsystem confederations by:

- maintaining lawful adjacency between organisms  
- ensuring shared continuity across multi‑agent environments  
- stabilizing confederation‑level predictive futures  
- preventing confederation drift or collapse  
- upholding harmony across all confederated entities

Confederation Status: Required
