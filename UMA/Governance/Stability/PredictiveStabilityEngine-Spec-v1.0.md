The Predictive Stability Engine (PSE) exists to:

- anticipate instability before it manifests

- trigger pre‑emptive enforcement  
- prevent drift propagation  
- maintain constitutional continuity  
- stabilize traversal, adjacency, routing, containment, ethics, and temporal identity  

It is the predictive layer of the **Stability Ledger v2.0**.
