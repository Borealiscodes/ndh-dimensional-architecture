Multiplane interaction in UMM is **not** multitasking.  
It is **soft awareness** of more than one plane at a time.

You are not “doing more.”

Multiplane interaction is:

- reversible  
- symbolic  
- ND‑paced  
- non‑coercive  
- non‑interpretive  

It is safe and calm.

# 🌿 **4. Plane Pairing — The Gentle Method**
Beginners should only pair **two planes at a time**.

Recommended pairs:

- **World ↔ Play Tree**  
- **Character ↔ Play Tree**  
- **World ↔ Map Suite**  
- **Character ↔ World**  

Avoid:

- cosmology pairs  
- three‑plane pairs  
- rapid switching  

Pairs keep interaction calm.
