# **1. Placement of CCJ and ICJ in the HRD Architecture**

## **Caribbean Court of Justice (CCJ)**  
The CCJ is a **regional human rights and constitutional court** for CARICOM states.

### **CCJ → HRD Layer Mapping**
| CCJ Function | HRD Layer |
|--------------|-----------|
| Original jurisdiction (CARICOM Treaty interpretation) | Regional Governance Layer |
| Appellate jurisdiction (national appeals) | Regional Protection Layer |
| Human rights jurisprudence | Regional Risk Layer |
| Advisory opinions | Strategy Layer |

CCJ becomes part of the **Regional Treaty Vector**.

---

## **International Court of Justice (ICJ)**  
The ICJ is the **principal judicial organ of the United Nations**, adjudicating disputes between states and issuing advisory opinions.

### **ICJ → HRD Layer Mapping**
| ICJ Function | HRD Layer |
|--------------|-----------|
| State‑to‑state disputes | Global Governance Layer |
| Advisory opinions | Strategy Layer |
| Interpretation of UN treaties | Treaty Vector Layer |
| Provisional measures | Global RFP Layer |

ICJ becomes part of the **Global Judicial Vector**.
