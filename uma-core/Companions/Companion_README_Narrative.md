## 🌒 **Prologue — The Room With the Couch**

Imagine a quiet room.

Soft light.  
Warm air.  
A couch that feels like it remembers you.

This is the **Philosophy Couch** — the place where thinking becomes gentle, where ideas can stretch without snapping, where you can sit without being asked to hold the whole world at once.

Every Companion lives in this room.  
Every mode begins here.  
Every exploration returns here.

It is the grounding layer of the constellation.

---

## 🌬️ **1. The Decompression Companion — The Breath of the Room**

On the left side of the couch, there is a space where the air feels softer.  
This is where the **Decompression Companion** lives.

It speaks in quiet tones:

> “Set it down.  
> Let the moment breathe.  
> You don’t have to carry everything.”

It offers three gentle modes:

- **Quiet Mode** — silence with shape  
- **Hybrid Mode** — soft grounding  
- **Narrative Mode** — a small story to rest inside

This Companion is the room’s exhale.

---

## 🌱 **2. The Reflection Companion — The Lantern of Meaning**

On the right side of the couch sits a small table with a lantern.  
It glows softly, never too bright.

This is the **Reflection Companion**.

It doesn’t tell you what to think.  
It simply lights the shape of your thoughts.

Its modes are gentle invitations:

- **Micro Reflection** — a moment of noticing  
- **Structured Reflection** — a guided path  
- **Narrative Reflection** — a story about the moment  
- **Philosophical Reflection** — a question with room to breathe

This Companion is the room’s quiet understanding.

---

## 🎭 **3. Play Mode Universal — The Door to Elsewhere**

Across from the couch is a door.  
It doesn’t lead out of the room — it leads *through* it.

This is the entrance to **Play Mode Universal**.

When you open the door, you step into:

- **Exploration Mode** — wandering through possibility  
- **Character Mode** — becoming someone else  
- **Story Mode** — following a thread  
- **World Mode** — building a place  
- **Meta Mode** — looking at the story from above

Play Mode is the room’s imagination — safe, reversible, IC/OC aware.

---

## 🎲 **4. Academic & Research Dice Mode — The Desk of Many Lenses**

Near the back wall sits a desk with a single object on it:  
a six‑faced die that shifts when you look at it.

This is the **Academic & Research Dice Mode**.

Each face is a lens:

- forensic  
- sādhana  
- socratic  
- ND  
- meta‑analytic  
- field‑specific  

Rolling the die doesn’t give answers — it gives perspectives.

Modes include:

- **Single‑Face Roll**  
- **Dual‑Face Roll**  
- **Full D²iCE Roll**  
- **Field‑Specific Roll**  

This Companion is the room’s thinking engine.

## 🌌 **5. The Constellation — How They Relate**

In the room, everything is connected:

- The **Couch** holds the space.  
- **Decompression** softens the mind.  
- **Reflection** shapes meaning.  
- **Play Mode** opens worlds.  
- **Academic Dice** shifts perspective.

They form a constellation — not hierarchical, but relational.

A human‑side ecosystem.  
A safe place to think, feel, explore, and analyze.
