# 🌱 **1. Continuity Principles**
Yearly continuity in UMM follows five constitutional principles:

- **Reversible** — continuity never locks you in  
- **Symbolic** — continuity is gentle, not literal  
- **ND‑paced** — continuity respects slow, predictable rhythms  
- **Non‑coercive** — continuity is optional, never required  
- **Non‑interpretive** — continuity is structural, not emotional  

These principles keep the year coherent without pressure.
