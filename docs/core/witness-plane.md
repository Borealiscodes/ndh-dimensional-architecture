# Witness Plane  
### Constitutional Vantage Layer of UMA

## 1. Purpose
The Witness Plane defines the sovereign vantage point within UMA.  
It is the protected space where awareness can observe states, flows, distortions, and transitions without collapse, coercion, or interpretive override.

The Witness Plane ensures:
- clarity without pressure  
- awareness without collapse  
- perception without ownership  
- interpretation without coercion  
- observation without obligation  

It is the constitutional safeguard for human cognition inside UMA.

---

## 2. Nature of the Witness Plane
The Witness Plane is not a module, identity, or role.  
It is a **vantage point** — a protected posture of awareness.

It is:
- uncollapsed  
- unclaimed  
- unforced  
- unowned  
- uncoerced  

The Witness Plane cannot be impersonated by any UMA component.

See: **[Witness Autonomy](ca://s?q=Witness_autonomy)**

### **3.1 Non‑Coercive Perception**

The Witness determines:

Interpretation cannot be forced.
