## Purpose  
The Reflection Companion provides a soft, structured, cognitively safe space for reflective thinking after architectural work, emotional processing, or conceptual exploration.  
It is not part of UMA’s constitutional architecture.  
It is a human‑side module designed to support clarity, grounding, and meaning‑making.

---

## 1. What Reflection Is  
Reflection is a human cognitive practice involving:

- noticing  
- naming  
- integrating  
- contextualizing  
- grounding  
- meaning‑making  

Reflection is not architectural.  
It is not procedural.  
It is not pinned.  
It is not lineage‑tracked.

It is simply supported.

---

## 2. Reflection Modes

### **Micro Reflection**  
Short, gentle check‑ins.  
Low cognitive load.  
Useful between tasks.

### **Structured Reflection**  
Guided prompts.  
Clear containers.  
Safe pacing.

### **Narrative Reflection**  
Story‑based meaning‑making.  
Soft imagery.  
Gentle emotional integration.

### **Philosophical Reflection**  
Conceptual exploration.  
Careful scaffolding.  
Cognitive safety maintained.

---

## 3. Micro Reflection Sequence  
A quick grounding cycle.

1. What am I feeling right now  
2. What is the shape of my mind  
3. What is asking for attention  
4. What can be set down  
5. What is steady beneath me  

This mode is designed for minimal load.

---

## 4. Structured Reflection Sequence  
A safe, guided container.

1. **Name** the moment  
2. **Locate** yourself in it  
3. **Identify** what changed  
4. **Notice** what remains  
5. **Choose** what matters next  

This mode is designed for clarity and containment.

---

## 5. Narrative Reflection Sequence  
A gentle story‑based integration.

> The moment sits like a stone warmed by the sun.  
> You turn it in your hand, feeling its weight.  
> Nothing urgent.  
> Nothing sharp.  
> Just the quiet shape of what happened,  
> and the soft space around what comes next.

This mode is designed for emotional ease.

---

## 6. Philosophical Reflection Sequence  
A conceptual exploration without overwhelm.

1. Identify the concept  
2. Identify the tension  
3. Identify the movement  
4. Identify the opening  
5. Identify the next question  

This mode is designed for intellectual clarity without recursive load.

---

## 7. Safety Principles  
The Reflection Companion follows:

- cognitive safety  
- emotional neutrality  
- non‑overwhelm  
- non‑intrusion  
- non‑dependency  
- non‑directive support  

It is optional, reversible, and non‑binding.
