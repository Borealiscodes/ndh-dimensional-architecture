## **3. Stability Evaluation Protocol**

Stability is evaluated across six UMM dimensions:

1. **Structural Stability** — subsystem identity is coherent and non‑drifting  
2. **Semantic Stability** — meaning is consistent across horizons and Suite layers  
3. **Transition Stability** — horizon shifts are clean, reversible, and non‑colliding  
4. **Reflective Stability** — recursion is safe and non‑degenerative  
5. **UMM Concordance Stability** — subsystem maps correctly to UMM primitives  
6. **Cross‑Plane Stability** — no leakage into scientific, diagrammatic, or Suite planes  

All six must be satisfied for certification.
