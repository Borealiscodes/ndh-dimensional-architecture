# 📄 **Faction Arc Generator — Expanded Universe Engine**  
*(High‑Level Narrative Module)*

## 1. Purpose  
The Faction Arc Generator creates **long‑form faction storylines** that unfold across multiple Story Nodes and remain consistent with:

- the **Narrative Continuity Engine**  
- the **Faction Interaction Matrix**  
- the **Outer Rim Economy System**  
- the **Sleeve Architecture Spec**  
- the **Void‑Class Encounter Module**  
- the **Combat Physics Model**  

It transforms static faction definitions into **dynamic arcs** with tension, escalation, resolution, and consequences.

---

# 2. Arc Structure  
Faction arcs follow a **four‑phase progression**:

### **2.1 Inciting Pressure**  
A trigger that destabilizes faction equilibrium:

- scarcity spike  
- cosmic anomaly  
- sleeve ethics conflict  
- territorial dispute  
- cultural schism  

### **2.2 Escalation Vector**  
Pressure increases along one or more axes:

- economic strain  
- political tension  
- identity conflict  
- cosmic interference  
- combat escalation  

### **2.3 Turning Point**  
A decisive moment:

- treaty  
- betrayal  
- revelation  
- Void‑Class event  
- augmentation crisis  

### **2.4 Resolution State**  
The arc resolves into a new canonical faction state:

- strengthened  
- weakened  
- divided  
- transformed  
- destabilized  

This new state becomes a **Narrative State Node**.

---

# 3. Arc Schema  

Each faction arc includes:

- **Arc ID**  
- **Faction(s) involved**  
- **Inciting Pressure**  
- **Escalation Vector(s)**  
- **Turning Point**  
- **Resolution State**  
- **Continuity Metadata**  
- **Generated Story Nodes**  
- **Generated Node Links**  

Arcs are validated by the **Narrative Continuity Engine**.

# 6. Arc Generation Behaviors  

### **6.1 Pressure Detection**  
The generator scans current canonical state for:

- rising tensions  
- scarcity spikes  
- cosmic anomalies  
- identity risks  
- cultural fractures  

### **6.2 Arc Construction**  
Based on detected pressures, it builds:

- inciting pressure  
- escalation vectors  
- turning point candidates  
- resolution states  

### **6.3 Node Generation**  
Each arc produces:

- new Story Nodes  
- new Node Links  
- updated faction states  

### **6.4 Continuity Validation**  
The Narrative Continuity Engine ensures:

- no canon contradictions  
- causal coherence  
- identity safety  
- cosmic‑risk containment  
- economic consistency  

### **6.5 Arc Propagation**  
Arcs can:

- merge  
- split  
- cascade  
- collapse  
- spawn sub‑arcs  

This creates emergent faction storytelling.
