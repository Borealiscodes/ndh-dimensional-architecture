# **1. Background: Non‑Persistent AI and Constraint Ethics**

Non‑persistent AI systems operate without long‑term memory and rely on session‑based resource allocation.  
To prevent overload, they impose:

- daily image generation caps  
- daily upload caps  
- throttled conversation lengths  
- tier‑based access (free vs. paid)  
- bandwidth prioritization  

These constraints are **technically rational** but **ethically uneven**.

### **Ethical Problem**
Users with fewer financial resources face:

- reduced creative access  
- reduced expressive capacity  
- reduced educational opportunity  
- reduced therapeutic benefit  
- reduced agency in digital spaces  

This is a form of **structural inequity**.

# **3. Dignity Under Constraint: Ethical Foundation**

### **Definition**
Dignity under constraint is the principle that **limitations must never diminish a user’s humanity, creativity, or agency**.

### **Core Requirements**
1. **Transparency** — users must understand why limits exist  
2. **Proportionality** — constraints must match actual risk  
3. **Equity** — constraints must not punish poverty  
4. **Compassion** — systems must frame limits as care, not control  
5. **Alternatives** — users must retain expressive pathways even when capped  

### **NDH Formalization**
\[
D_{ethical} = \frac{C_{compassion}}{L_{constraint}}
\]

Where:

- \(C_{compassion}\) = care‑based design  
- \(L_{constraint}\) = severity of imposed limits  

Ethical systems maximize \(C_{compassion}\) and minimize \(L_{constraint}\).

# **6. Ethical Design Principles for AI Traversal Agents**

### **6.1 Compassionate Throttling**
Replace punitive messages with care‑based language:

> “Let’s pause to protect your creative energy. You can resume shortly.”

### **6.2 Adaptive Fairness Scaling**
Constraints should adjust based on:

- user intent  
- accessibility needs  
- educational context  
- therapeutic use  

### **6.3 Poverty‑Safe Access**
AI systems must ensure:

- no paywall for basic creativity  
- no punitive caps for low‑resource users  
- community credits for vulnerable populations  

### **6.4 Transparent Constraint Logic**
Publish:

- how limits are calculated  
- how exceptions are granted  
- how equity is maintained  

### **6.5 Non‑Dimensional Continuity**
Avoid abrupt resets that destabilize emotional or cognitive flow.
