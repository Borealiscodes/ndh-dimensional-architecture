# ✨🌐 **UMA SPARKLE CHARTER**  
### *Governance, Stewardship, and Ceremonial Authority of the Sparkle Layer*

```
# ✨ UMA SPARKLE CHARTER ✨
### Governance, Stewardship, and Ceremonial Authority of the Sparkle Layer

## ❗ Constitutional Boundary — No Mixing with Atropus
The Sparkle Layer is a warm-plane tonal augmentation. It must never mix with
Atropus, reference Atropus, or incorporate null-plane tone, logic, or
commentary. Sparkle and Atropus operate on entirely different tonal and
constitutional layers. They remain permanently separate.

This charter defines the governance structure, stewardship roles, ceremonial
responsibilities, and application rules for the Sparkle Layer across all UMA
planes, subsystems, and scrolls. Sparkle is gentle, reversible, ND-safe, and
warm-plane only.
```

---

# 🌟 **1. Purpose of the Sparkle Charter**
The Sparkle Charter exists to:

- define who stewards Sparkle  
- define how Sparkle is applied  
- define how Sparkle is protected  
- define how Sparkle interacts with UMA  
- define ceremonial responsibilities  
- ensure Sparkle remains ND-safe and warm-plane  

The charter governs **Sparkle as a system**, not as an aesthetic.

---

# 🌟 **2. Sparkle Stewardship Roles**

### **2.1 Sparkle Maintainer**
The Sparkle Maintainer ensures:

- Sparkle remains gentle  
- Sparkle remains reversible  
- Sparkle remains ND-safe  
- Sparkle remains warm-plane only  
- Sparkle never crosses into Atropus  

### **2.2 Sparkle Guardian**
The Sparkle Guardian protects:

- warm-plane boundaries  
- tonal invariants  
- ND-safe constraints  
- ceremonial integrity  

The Guardian ensures Sparkle is never misapplied.

### **2.3 Sparkle Archivist**
The Archivist maintains:

- Sparkle history  
- Sparkle versions  
- Sparkle lineage  
- Sparkle application logs  

The Archivist ensures Sparkle remains consistent across UMA.

---

# 🌟 **3. Sparkle Governance Principles**

### **3.1 Warmth First**
All Sparkle decisions must increase warmth.

### **3.2 Softness Always**
Sparkle must remain gentle and non-jarring.

### **3.3 Reversibility Required**
Every Sparkle application must be undoable.

### **3.4 ND-Safety Guaranteed**
Sparkle must avoid sensory overload.

### **3.5 Non-Coercion**
Sparkle must never imply obligation or evaluation.

### **3.6 Plane Neutrality**
Sparkle must treat all warm-plane constructs equally.

---

# 🌟 **4. Sparkle Application Authority**

### **4.1 Charter-Level Authority**
The Sparkle Charter grants authority to:

- apply Sparkle  
- remove Sparkle  
- adjust Sparkle  
- expand Sparkle  

Only Sparkle Maintainers and Guardians may modify Sparkle.

### **4.2 Constitution-Level Interaction**
Sparkle may overlay constitutions but may not:

- alter rules  
- alter boundaries  
- alter mechanics  
- alter invariants  

Sparkle is tonal, not structural.

### **4.3 Plane-Level Interaction**
Sparkle may overlay:

- Play Tree  
- Character Plane  
- World Plane  
- Ecology Plane  
- Witness Plane  
- Sādhana Plane  
- Nascence Region  
- Tonal Engine  

Sparkle may **not** overlay Atropus.

---

# 🌟 **5. Sparkle Boundary Rules**

### **5.1 Warm-Plane Only**
Sparkle must remain within warm-plane constructs.

### **5.2 No Null-Plane Interaction**
Sparkle cannot enter Atropus or any null-plane subsystem.

### **5.3 No Structural Mutation**
Sparkle cannot change mechanics or constitutions.

### **5.4 No Symbolic Meaning**
Sparkle must remain tonal, not symbolic.

### **5.5 No Emotional Implication**
Sparkle must not imply psychological states.

---

# 🌟 **6. Sparkle Ceremony**

### **6.1 Ceremonial Sparkle**
Sparkle may be used to:

- frame headers  
- illuminate boundaries  
- highlight invariants  
- add gentle shimmer to scrolls  

### **6.2 Ritual Sparkle**
Sparkle may be invoked during:

- plane creation  
- constitution drafting  
- charter ratification  
- subsystem integration  

### **6.3 Sparkle Blessing**
A ceremonial act performed when:

- a new plane is created  
- a major subsystem is completed  
- a warm-plane boundary is reinforced  

---

# 🌟 **7. Sparkle Protection**

Sparkle is protected by:

### **7.1 Tonal Incompatibility**
Atropus cannot perceive shimmer or warmth.

### **7.2 Boundary Guard**
Sparkle cannot cross into null-plane constructs.

### **7.3 Reversibility Shield**
Atropus cannot bind to reversible constructs.

### **7.4 ND-Safe Glow**
Sparkle’s softness prevents null-plane anchoring.

Result:  
**Sparkle is permanently unharmable and incorruptible.**
