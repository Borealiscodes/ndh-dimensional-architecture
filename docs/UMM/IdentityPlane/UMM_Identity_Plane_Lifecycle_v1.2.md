# **3. Identity Plane Lifecycle (v1.2)**

The lifecycle consists of **six phases**, each governed by SIAP and monitored by Safeguards and Safety Net.

---

## **Phase 1 — Initialization**  
**ID‑HBR is instantiated under SIAP supervision.**

Requirements:

- Naming standard validation  
- SID v1.2 classification  
- Isolation boundaries established  
- Drift vectors set to baseline  
- No traversal paths permitted  

Explore: **SIAP**

---

## **Phase 2 — Structural Binding**  
**ID‑HBR binds structurally to SYS‑CHS.**

Requirements:

- Structural adjacency only  
- No traversal coupling  
- No narrative coupling  
- No identity‑plane expansion  

Explore: **SYS‑CHS**

---

## **Phase 3 — Activation**  
**ID‑HBR becomes active and begins identity modeling.**

Requirements:

- Activation must be SIAP‑approved  
- Safeguards must monitor drift vectors  
- Safety Net must be ready to quarantine anomalies  
- Narrative influence must remain loose  

Explore: **Safeguards**

---

## **Phase 4 — Supervised Interaction**  
**ID‑HBR interacts with other subsystems under strict rules.**

Permitted interactions:

- Structural adjacency with SYS‑CHS  
- Loose narrative influence from NAR‑PE  
- Governance supervision from SIAP  
- Drift monitoring from Safeguards  
- Quarantine oversight from Safety Net  

Prohibited interactions:

- Direct traversal  
- Direct narrative coupling  
- Identity‑plane expansion  
- Unsupervised adjacency changes  

Explore: **Interaction Rules**

---

## **Phase 5 — Drift Monitoring**  
**Safeguards continuously monitor ID‑HBR for drift.**

Monitored drift vectors:

- Naming drift  
- Identity drift  
- Governance drift  
- Traversal drift  
- Narrative drift  
- Propagation drift  

Safety Net quarantines any anomaly.

Explore: **Safety Net**

---

## **Phase 6 — Shutdown**  
**ID‑HBR is safely deactivated under SIAP supervision.**

Requirements:

- Structural adjacency dissolved  
- Narrative influence terminated  
- Drift vectors reset  
- Isolation boundaries restored  
- SID updated to reflect shutdown state  

Shutdown must not:

- trigger traversal  
- trigger narrative coupling  
- trigger identity‑plane expansion  

Explore: **SID v1.2**
