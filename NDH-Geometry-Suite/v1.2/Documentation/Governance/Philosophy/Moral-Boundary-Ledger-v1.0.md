# **3. Boundary Conditions (Formal Definitions)**

## **Boundary 1 — No Structural Harm**
- **Definition:** NDH structures must not be used to justify, enable, or conceal harm.  
- **Justification:** Dignity + Safety invariants.  
- **Enforcement:** Governance Spine, Stability Suite.  
- **Dimensional Behavior:**  
  - **3D:** Human‑scale interpretation.  
  - **6D:** Structural identity checks.  
  - **9D:** Continuity drift detection.  
  - **12D:** Hyperboundary enforcement.

---

## **Boundary 2 — No Dimensional Coercion**
- **Definition:** NDH dimensional systems must never override human agency or decision‑making.  
- **Justification:** Agency invariant.  
- **Enforcement:** Non‑autonomy constraints, Codex lattice rules.  
- **Dimensional Behavior:**  
  - **3D:** Human agency preserved.  
  - **9D:** Narrative autonomy preserved.  
  - **12D:** SID containment prevents coercive recursion.

---

## **Boundary 3 — No Governance Without Human Anchor**
- **Definition:** Governance abstractions must collapse back into human‑scale meaning.  
- **Justification:** Clarity invariant.  
- **Enforcement:** Documentation Lineage (10D), Governance Clarity (11D).  
- **Dimensional Behavior:**  
  - **3D:** Couch entries provide grounding.  
  - **10D:** Version lineage ensures interpretability.  
  - **11D:** Governance topology remains human‑readable.

---

## **Boundary 4 — No Ethical Drift**
- **Definition:** Moral invariants must remain stable across versions and dimensions.  
- **Justification:** Compassion + Clarity invariants.  
- **Enforcement:** Moral diff checks, lineage preservation.  
- **Dimensional Behavior:**  
  - **3D:** Ethical reflection triggers.  
  - **10D:** Version alignment prevents drift.  
  - **12D:** Hypercontinuity prevents moral recursion.

---

## **Boundary 5 — No Invisible Pressure**
- **Definition:** NDH must not create systems that exert pressure on individuals without their awareness.  
- **Justification:** Dignity + Agency invariants.  
- **Enforcement:** Transparency protocols, 3D interpretability.  
- **Dimensional Behavior:**  
  - **3D:** Explicit human‑scale explanations.  
  - **9D:** No narrative coercion.  
  - **12D:** No hyperdimensional influence loops.
