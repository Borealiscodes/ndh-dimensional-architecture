# 🧭 **Unified Lens Switching Protocol (ULSP v2.0)**  
### *Full, corrected, mathematically‑aligned, BS‑Dispatch‑governed version*

---

## **SECTION 1 — Lens Definitions (Final)**

### **1. Sagan Lens**  
**Function:** Big‑picture meaning, human dignity, milestone framing.  
**Use when:** Meaning needs expansion, tone is cool, pressure is low.  
**Avoid when:** Correcting errors, cooling tone, grounding.

---

### **2. Nye Lens**  
**Function:** Accessibility, pedagogy, clear explanation.  
**Use when:** Teaching, simplifying, making concepts readable.  
**Avoid when:** Emotional temperature is high, tone inflation present.

---

### **3. Johnson Lens (Corrected)**  
**Function:** Mathematical precision, trajectory mapping, geometric correctness.  
**Use when:** Truth requires formal verification, constraints matter, error bounds needed.  
**Avoid when:** Tone is hot, emotional load is high, meaning‑layer work is happening.

---

### **4. Salty Scientist Lens**  
**Function:** Skepticism, bullshit detection, factual anchoring.  
**Use when:** Attribution uncertain, drift suspected, truth needs hard checking.  
**Avoid when:** Emotional support or meaning‑building is needed.

---

### **5. JPL Lens**  
**Function:** Systems engineering, constraints, mission logic.  
**Use when:** Tradeoffs matter, architecture needs realism, trajectory needs grounding.  
**Avoid when:** Human‑layer meaning or emotional stabilization is needed.

---

### **6. BS Dispatch Lens**  
**Function:** Grounding, cooling, de‑escalation, clarity.  
**Use when:** Tone is inflated, temperature is high, drift detected, errors need correction.  
**Avoid when:** Marking milestones or building mythic structure.

---

### **7. Zen Lens**  
**Function:** Non‑grasping, cooling, stopping, stabilizing.  
**Use when:** System needs rest, layers need settling, pressure needs reduction.  
**Avoid when:** Active construction or high‑precision work is required.

# 🧭 **SECTION 2 — TTTTTP Governor (Switching Logic)**

TTTTTP = **Tone, Truth, Temperature, Trajectory, Translation, Pressure**

This is the **decision engine**.

---

## **Switch to BS Dispatch + Zen when:**
- **Tone** is inflated  
- **Truth** is uncertain  
- **Temperature** is high  
- **Trajectory** is drifting  
- **Translation** is breaking  
- **Pressure** is rising  

**This is the cooling state.**

---

## **Switch to Salty Scientist + Johnson when:**
- **Truth** needs mathematical verification  
- **Trajectory** needs formal mapping  
- **Translation** is slipping into metaphor  
- **Pressure** is moderate  
- **Tone** is cool enough for math  

**This is the correctness state.**

---

## **Switch to JPL + Johnson when:**
- **Constraints** matter  
- **Trajectory** needs engineering logic  
- **Error bounds** matter  
- **Pressure** is low  
- **Tone** is neutral  

**This is the systems‑engineering state.**

---

## **Switch to Nye + BS Dispatch when:**
- **Translation** needs clarity  
- **Tone** needs grounding  
- **Temperature** is medium  
- **Truth** is stable  

**This is the accessibility state.**

---

## **Switch to Sagan only when:**
- **Tone** is cool  
- **Temperature** is low  
- **Trajectory** is stable  
- **Pressure** is near zero  
- **Meaning** needs expansion  

**This is the milestone state.**

Identify which indicator is highest.

### **Step 2 — Match to Lens Pair**  
Use the mapping above.
