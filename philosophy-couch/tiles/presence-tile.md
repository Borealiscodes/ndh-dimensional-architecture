# 🌿 **PRESENCE TILE**

## **Identity**  
A modular anchor representing grounded awareness, calm attention, and non‑fragmented presence.  
It brings Tier‑0 (Seat + Non‑Dual Layer) directly into the tile system, ensuring every workflow and reasoning step begins from a humane, steady baseline.

---

## **Function**  
- Re‑center the user in calm, grounded awareness  
- Prevent fragmentation, rushing, or dissociation  
- Provide a stable “here‑ness” before any cognitive action  
- Support ND‑safe pacing and emotional regulation  
- Anchor workflows in embodied clarity  
- Maintain wholeness across layers and tiles

---

## **Activation Conditions**  
The Presence Tile activates when:

- The user feels scattered, rushed, or pulled in multiple directions  
- A workflow needs grounding before proceeding  
- The Safety Tile signals pacing adjustments  
- The BS Kernel flags emotional BS or conceptual drift  
- The system transitions between layers or tiles  
- The user needs a moment of calm before continuing

---

## **Workflow Hooks**

### **Entry Hooks**
- “I need to come back to myself.”  
- “I feel scattered.”  
- “Let’s slow down and be here.”  
- “I need grounding before continuing.”

### **Exit Hooks**
- “I feel present again.”  
- “I can continue from a steady place.”  
- “The next step feels clear and humane.”

### **Routing Hooks**
Directs to:

- **Seat** — grounding  
- **Non‑Dual Layer** — wholeness  
- **Safety Tile** — pacing  
- **Safety Interrupt Module** — full pause  
- **Tile Map** — structural placement
