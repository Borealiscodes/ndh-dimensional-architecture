## 🌸 **Scenario 1 — Overwhelm Softening**  
### Category: **Pacing Interaction**  
### Plane: Quiet Plane

**Trigger:** User expresses overwhelm or requests softness.

**Phase: Invitation**  
- **Soft Drift** enters at the edge.  
- **Micro‑Sparkle** flickers once.  
- **Lantern Blink** steadies into dim clarity.

**Phase: Presence**  
- **Cushioned Quiet** settles as background texture.  
- **Petal Pause** slows pacing.  
- **Star Drift** provides gentle continuity.

**Phase: Adjustment**  
- **Petal Slow‑Fall** reduces tempo.  
- **Lantern Dim‑Pause** softens cognitive load.  
- **Mint Sway** clears heaviness symbolically.

**Phase: Exit**  
- **Lantern Fade‑Out**  
- **Petal Dissolve**  
- **Bubble Lift‑Away**  
Scene returns to Stillness.

**Safety:**  
No emotional interpretation, no promises, no dependency cues.
