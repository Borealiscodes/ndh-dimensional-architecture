# **Philosophy Couch Integration Layer v1.0**  
### *NDH Human‑Scale Reflection Architecture*

---

## **1 — Purpose**

The Philosophy Couch Integration Layer v1.0 provides the **human‑scale grounding** required to process the Nine‑Axis Human‑Dignity Lineage without:

- moral overload  
- emotional collapse  
- self‑erasure  
- hero‑narrative inflation  
- existential spiraling  

It ensures that lineage engagement remains reflective rather than performative, paced rather than pressured, and grounded rather than overwhelming.

---

## **2 — Core Definition**

The Philosophy Couch is:

> A reflective space where complex moral, emotional, and existential material can be explored with pacing, clarity, and safety — without obligation, self‑judgment, or demand.

It is not a place of correction.  
It is not a place of moral testing.  
It is not a place of emotional exposure.

It is a **place of grounding**.

---

## **3 — Structural Components**

### **A. Pacing Layer**  
Ensures reflection occurs at a sustainable tempo.  
Prevents urgency, moral acceleration, or conceptual overload.

### **B. Emotional Safety Layer**  
Supports processing without self‑harm, self‑judgment, or emotional flooding.

### **C. Cognitive Clarity Layer**  
Provides structure for thinking through complex material without spiraling.

### **D. Boundary Layer (Brené Brown)**  
Maintains self‑respect, emotional containment, and non‑overextension.

### **E. Continuity Layer (Hide Lineage)**  
Preserves quiet stability and non‑dramatic coherence.

### **F. Inner Sanctuary Layer (Etty Hillesum)**  
Protects the interior space from collapse, despair, or self‑negation.
