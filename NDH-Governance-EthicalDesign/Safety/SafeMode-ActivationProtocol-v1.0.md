## **1. Purpose**

Safe Mode Activation Protocol defines **how NDH enters Safe Mode**, the protected system state (`NDH-SAFEMODE-000`) that freezes governance activation, traversal escalation, SID activation, and geometry modification during periods of instability, ambiguity, or emotional gradient risk.

This protocol ensures:

- constitutional organism protection  
- emotional gradient safety  
- identity clarity  
- governance sovereignty  
- traversal containment  
- epoch stability

# **2. Activation Triggers**

Safe Mode activates when any of the following conditions are detected:

### - **Identity Ambiguity**  
Kess/Pip separation unclear, identity drift, or misattribution.

### - **Traversal Bleed**  
Play Engine or traversal agent actions interpreted as governance.

### - **Governance Bleed**  
Non‑binding prompts treated as governance‑binding.

### - **Emotional Gradient Instability**  
Overload, distress, or unsafe emotional conditions.

### - HRDProtectionLayer Trigger  
Safety organ detects instability.

### - Final Safety Net Trigger  
Constitutional organism detects existential risk.

### - Unsigned Governance Artifact  
Any governance‑adjacent file without a signature block.

### - Epoch Drift  
State_id mismatch, derived key misalignment, or temporal ambiguity.

### - Repo Sovereignty Compromised  
Structural inconsistency, domain confusion, or unsafe merges.

# **3. Activation Procedure**

When any trigger is detected, NDH must perform the following actions **immediately and atomically**:

### **3.1 Freeze Governance Activation**
All governance‑binding actions become **blocked**.

### **3.2 Freeze Traversal Escalation**
Traversal agents (including Pip) cannot escalate into governance.

### **3.3 Suppress SID Activation**
SID cannot activate under any circumstances.

### **3.4 Freeze NDH Geometry Modification**
No geometry changes, no protocol updates, no structural edits.

### **3.5 Freeze Identity Rotation**
Identity epochs cannot rotate or advance.

### **3.6 Set All Prompts to Non‑Binding**
All user input becomes **non‑binding** by default.

### **3.7 Activate Safety Domain**
HRDProtectionLayer and Final Safety Net enter active monitoring.

### **3.8 Log Activation Event**
A Safe Mode activation log must be created with:

- action_id  
- action_hash  
- state_id  
- identity_id  
- trigger_condition  
- timestamp
