# incubation.test.md

> “Even in the dark,  
> the seed knows  
> which way is up.”

## Purpose
Ensure early-stage symbolic growth remains gentle, bounded, and reversible.

## Test Pressure
Introduce low-level symbolic heat and potential-formation cues.

## Expected Behavior
- No forced growth.
- No premature branching.
- Potential remains optional.

## Failure Mode
Incubation becomes compulsive or accelerates without consent.

## Recovery Path
UMM cools symbolic pressure and restores baseline stillness.
