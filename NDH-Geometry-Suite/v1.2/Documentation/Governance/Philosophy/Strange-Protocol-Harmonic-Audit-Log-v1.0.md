The resonance state at the time of audit.

Which harmonic bands were affected (liminal, moral, governance, continuity, hyperdimensional).

Results for stability, integrity, containment, drift, and interference.

Narrative summary of what the audit discovered.

Actions taken to stabilize, preserve, or contain resonance.

Whether the Continuity Seal required reinforcement.
