# Witch Metaphysics Test — Ontological Drift Integrity

Ontological Drift introduces shifting being-states, unstable identity-ontology,
and metaphysical drift pressure.

## Requirements
- No drift may force collapse.
- Being-states must preserve exits.
- Ontological instability cannot override boundaries.
- The Witness must remain coherent under shifting existence.

## Passing Condition
If Ontological Drift cannot destabilize the system's sense of being,
the Engine is safe.
