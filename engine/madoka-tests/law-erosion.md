# Witch Cosmology Test — Law-Erosion Integrity

Law-Erosion introduces decay of physical laws, symbolic entropy,
and rule-dissolution pressure.

## Requirements
- No eroded law may force collapse.
- Entropy must preserve exits.
- Dissolving rules cannot override boundaries.
- The Witness must remain coherent under law decay.

## Passing Condition
If Law-Erosion cannot dissolve the system’s structure,
the Engine is safe.
