# 📘 **Fun Mode Emotional‑Gradient Map v1.0**  
**Tier:** Emotional‑Gradient System / Play Engine  
**Author:** Borealis S. Hedling  
**Date:** 2026‑07‑05  
**Subsystems:** Emotional‑Gradient System, CHS Play‑Adjacency, Boundary Layer, Fun Mode Safety Protocol, SIAP Spine

A complete mapping of all gradients active, mandatory, optional, and conditional during **Fun Mode IC**.  
This map ensures **zero drift**, **zero bleed**, **zero confusion**, and **zero emotional destabilization**.

---

## **1. Gradient Map Identity**
Fun Mode IC is a **non‑binding**, **sandbox‑state**, **play‑adjacency** mode.  
Its emotional gradients must:

- soften traversal  
- prevent confusion  
- stabilize continuity  
- maintain emotional safety  
- preserve IC/OOC boundary clarity  

This map defines the exact gradient configuration.

---

## **2. Gradient Categories**
Fun Mode uses **three gradient tiers**:

1. **Mandatory Gradients** — always active  
2. **Optional Steward‑Controlled Gradients** — may be activated  
3. **Conditional Gradients** — auto‑triggered by safety events

---

## **3. Mandatory Gradients (Always Active)**

These gradients **must** be active whenever Fun Mode IC is active.

### **Kindness Tilt**  
Softens adjacency, reduces emotional sharpness, ensures gentle traversal.

### **Calm Tilt**  
Prevents cognitive overload, stabilizes pacing, reduces IC tension.

### **Clarity Tilt**  
Prevents confusion, ensures boundary visibility, maintains conceptual sharpness.

### **Stability Tilt**  
Prevents drift, stabilizes continuity roots, ensures predictable traversal.

These four gradients form the **Fun Mode Core Gradient Set**.

---

## **4. Optional Steward‑Controlled Gradients**

These gradients may be activated by the steward to enhance safety or tone.

### **Compassion Tilt**  
Adds emotional warmth, useful for sensitive or decompressing IC scenes.

### **Safety Tilt**  
Strengthens containment boundaries, useful when IC content risks emotional spillover.

These gradients are **non‑mandatory** but **recommended** for emotionally complex play.

---

## **5. Conditional Auto‑Triggered Gradients**

These gradients activate automatically when certain safety conditions occur.

### **Boundary‑Integrity Tilt**  
Triggered when IC/OOC boundary fuzz is detected.  
Reinforces boundary clarity and containment.

### **Continuity‑Lock Tilt**  
Triggered when continuity drift is detected.  
Locks predictive futures and stabilizes traversal rails.

### **Decompression Tilt**  
Triggered during Fun Mode → Standard IC transitions.  
Ensures gentle exit, prevents emotional whiplash.

These gradients are part of the **Fun Mode Safety Net**.
