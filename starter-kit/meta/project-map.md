# Project Map  
### A Simple, Friendly Overview of Your Project’s Components

## 1. Purpose
The Project Map gives you a calm, high-level overview of your project.  
It shows the main areas, how they fit together, and where future growth can happen.

This map is descriptive, not prescriptive.  
It is a gentle guide, not a demand.

---

## 2. Core Area
The core area contains the foundational identity of your project.

Examples:
- core-charter.md  
- core-principles.md  
- core-boundaries.md  

This area answers:  
“What is the heart of the project?”

---

## 3. Workflow Area
The workflow area contains templates and guides for how you work.

Examples:
- top-level-workflow-template.md

This area answers:  
“How do I move through the project?”
