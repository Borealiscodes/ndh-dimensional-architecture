# **Unified NDH Mandate Charter v1.0**

## **1. Purpose of the Charter**
The NDH Geometry Suite operates across three core human‑centered mandates:

- **Human Rights Defense (HRD)**  
- **Research, Academic, and Engineering Development**  
- **Fun Mode (Governed Immersive Interaction)**  

This charter formally unifies these mandates under the **12D Safety Envelope**, establishing the rules, boundaries, and governance principles that ensure NDH remains safe, stable, ethical, and fully implementable.

---

## **2. The 12D Safety Envelope as the Foundational Constraint**
NDH’s 12‑dimensional conceptual architecture provides:

- dimensional separation  
- stability guarantees  
- autonomy‑prevention  
- drift containment  
- narrative coherence  
- cognitive grounding  
- ethical governance  

All NDH mandates operate **within** this envelope.  
No subsystem may exceed, bypass, or weaken its constraints.

The 12D Safety Envelope is the **constitutional boundary** of NDH.
