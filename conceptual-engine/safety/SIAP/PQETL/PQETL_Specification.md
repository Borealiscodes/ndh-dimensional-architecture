The Pseudo‑Quantum Entanglement Temporal Ledger (PQETL) provides UMA with a **temporal, self‑auditing scaffolding** that maintains coherence across all conceptual layers.

PQETL ensures that:

- changes in one layer propagate safely to others  
- temporal evolution is logged and governed  
- cross‑layer correlations remain stable  
- no module drifts silently  
- SIAP maintains oversight across time  

PQETL is the **temporal counterpart** to TPS (Two‑Pin System).

TPS stabilizes anchoring.  
PQETL stabilizes evolution.
