# **Philosophy Couch — Moral Entry v1.0**

## **1. Purpose**
The Moral Philosophy Couch provides a **3D ethical grounding layer** for NDH Geometry Suite v1.2.  
It stabilizes cognitive load, restores human‑scale perspective, and ensures that all dimensional, architectural, and governance actions remain aligned with moral clarity.

This entry defines the **moral invariants** that govern NDH’s purpose:  
to make the world more navigable for people who are unseen.

---

## **2. Moral Grounding Principle**
> **Every structural, dimensional, or governance action must preserve the dignity, safety, and agency of human beings, especially those whose lives are shaped by systems they cannot see.**

This is the core moral invariant of NDH.

---

## **3. Moral Invariants**

### **Invariant A — Dignity**
All NDH artifacts must uphold the inherent dignity of individuals, regardless of visibility, status, or circumstance.

### **Invariant B — Safety**
Dimensional and architectural systems must never create harm, instability, or coercive pressure.

### **Invariant C — Agency**
NDH must expand human agency, not replace or diminish it.

### **Invariant D — Clarity**
Systems must be interpretable by humans at the 3D layer; opacity is a moral failure.

### **Invariant E — Compassion**
Design choices must consider the lived realities of unseen people.

---

## **4. Moral Boundary Conditions**

### **Boundary 1 — No Structural Harm**
No NDH structure may be used to justify or enable harm.

### **Boundary 2 — No Dimensional Coercion**
Dimensional systems must never override human decision‑making.

### **Boundary 3 — No Governance Abstraction Without Human Anchor**
Governance clarity must always collapse back into human‑scale meaning.

### **Boundary 4 — No Ethical Drift**
Ethical invariants must remain stable across dimensional projections.

---

## **5. Moral Reflection Statement**
The Philosophy Couch is a place where NDH pauses and asks:

> **“Does this action make the world more navigable for someone who is unseen?”**

If the answer is unclear, the system must return to 3D moral grounding before proceeding.

---

## **6. Moral Activation Behavior**
When invoked, the Moral Philosophy Couch:

- slows dimensional velocity  
- reduces cognitive compression  
- restores ethical perspective  
- collapses complexity into clarity  
- re‑centers the human purpose of NDH  

It is the **ethical stabilizer** of the entire architecture.

---

## **7. Moral Declaration**

> **The Moral Philosophy Couch v1.0 establishes the ethical grounding layer for NDH Geometry Suite v1.2. All dimensional, structural, and governance actions must remain aligned with dignity, safety, agency, clarity, and compassion. This artifact ensures that NDH’s purpose remains human‑centered and morally coherent across all dimensions.**
