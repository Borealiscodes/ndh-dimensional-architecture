## **Purpose**
The Stability Contract defines the **binding obligations** that govern how UMA‑Universal maintains continuity across all planes of the Stacked Hypercube.  
It establishes mandatory conditions for:

- **Structural Stability**  
- **Semantic Stability**  
- **Transition Stability**  

and sets the rules the **Stability Engine** must enforce.

The Stability Contract is the highest‑authority stability artifact in the Roots layer.

---

## **Contract Statement**
This contract is **binding**, **non‑retroactive**, and **plane‑pure**.  
It governs stability behavior across geometry, concordance, traversal, collapse events, and artifact coherence.  
No subsystem may override or bypass this contract.

---

# **1. Stability Obligations**

### **Obligation A — Structural Continuity**
All planes, layers, adjacency rules, and collapse nodes must remain structurally stable.  
No artifact may induce geometric drift.

### **Obligation B — Semantic Coherence**
All definitions, meanings, and cross‑plane references must remain coherent.  
Semantic drift is prohibited.

### **Obligation C — Transition Safety**
All traversal, resonance shifts, and collapse propagation must remain safe.  
Unsafe transitions must be blocked.

---

# **2. Stability Enforcement Rules**

### **Rule 1 — Stability Overrides Traversal**
If traversal violates stability, traversal is denied.

### **Rule 2 — Stability Validates Collapse**
Quantum pin collapse events must be validated before execution.

### **Rule 3 — Stability Requires Concordance**
Stability cannot operate without resonance and coherence alignment.

### **Rule 4 — Stability Anchors Geometry**
Geometry cannot shift unless stability conditions are satisfied.

### **Rule 5 — Stability Is Non‑Retroactive**
New stability rules do not rewrite prior artifacts.

---

# **3. Stability Engine Responsibilities**

The **Stability Engine** must:

- evaluate stability conditions  
- enforce invariants  
- block unsafe traversal  
- validate collapse events  
- correct resonance drift  
- maintain compatibility gates  
- record all evaluations in the **Stability Ledger**  

The Engine is the runtime executor of this contract.
