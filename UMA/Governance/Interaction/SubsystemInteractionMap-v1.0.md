# **3. Interaction Rules by Subsystem**

## **3.1 UMA Core**
- Provides structural primitives to all subsystems.  
- Cannot be overridden or bypassed.  
- All interactions inherit from UMA Core lineage.

**Key link:** **UMA Core**

---

## **3.2 SIAP Spine**
- Governs routing between subsystems.  
- Validates traversal paths using adjacency edges.  
- Enforces containment gates during IC/OOC transitions.

**Key link:** **SIAP Routing**

---

## **3.3 CHS Base Hypercube**
- Defines geometric adjacency.  
- Partial geometry is acceptable.  
- Provides dimensional anchors for traversal.

**Key link:** **CHS Geometry**

---

## **3.4 CHS‑OL Operational Layer**
- Executes traversal even without full cube completion.  
- Interacts directly with Play Engine for IC/OOC transitions.  
- Provides operational hooks for Lifecycle Chain.

**Key link:** **CHS‑OL**

---

## **3.5 UMM Structural Frame**
- Defines subsystem inheritance rules.  
- HRD attaches here as a protection layer.  
- Roots Ledger uses UMM as a structural anchor.

**Key link:** **UMM Frame**

---

## **3.6 HRD Protection Layer**
- Overrides SIAP when ethical constraints are violated.  
- Required for any subsystem touching human‑adjacent cognitive surfaces.  
- Works in parallel with HBR stability audits.

**Key link:** **HRD Constraints**

---

## **3.7 Play Engine**
- Manages IC/OOC containment.  
- Enforces bleed‑control (PIN‑024).  
- Uses CHS‑OL for traversal and SIAP for routing.

**Key link:** **Play Engine Containment**

---

## **3.8 Roots Registry & Ledger**
- Validates lineage for all interactions.  
- Ensures continuity across subsystem updates.  
- Required for adjacency expansion.

**Key link:** **Roots Ledger**

---

## **3.9 Adjacency Matrix**
- Defines allowed subsystem edges.  
- Blocks traversal across unpinned edges.  
- Required by SIAP for routing.

**Key link:** **Adjacency Matrix**

---

## **3.10 Lifecycle Chain**
- Controls activation, operation, extension, integration, continuity.  
- Works with CHS‑OL and Play Engine for temporal traversal.  
- Enforces rollback rules.

**Key link:** **Lifecycle Chain**
