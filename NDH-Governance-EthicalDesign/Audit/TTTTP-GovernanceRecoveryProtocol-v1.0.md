## **1. Purpose**

The Governance Recovery Protocol defines how NDH governance returns to stability after a structural failure, identity ambiguity, or traversal bleed event — such as the Play Engine Fiasco.

It ensures:

- governance sovereignty  
- identity clarity  
- emotional gradient safety

This protocol contains **no private keys**, **no entropy**, and **no sensitive material**.
