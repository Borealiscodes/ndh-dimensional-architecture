## **1. Purpose**

The Constitutional Amendment Register defines and records the **entire lifecycle** of constitutional amendments within UMA‑Universal.  
It ensures that constitutional evolution is:

- stable  
- traceable  
- drift‑neutral  
- governed by SIAP  
- validated by CI  
- protected by Safeguards  
- quarantined by Safety Net when necessary  

This register is **append‑only**, **immutable**, and **sovereign**.

---

## **2. Amendment Pipeline (v1.0)**

Every amendment must pass through the following pipeline:

1. **Proposal (SIAP only)**  
2. **Safeguards Review**  
3. **Safety Net Approval**  
4. **CI Validation**  
5. **Ratification**  
6. **Ledger Binding**  
7. **Versioning & Publication**

This pipeline is now constitutionally required and cannot be bypassed.

## **4. Amendment Categories**

### **Rights Amendments**
Modify, expand, or clarify rights across subsystem, traversal, identity, narrative, governance, or integration planes.

### **Duties Amendments**
Modify operational obligations, enforcement rules, or lifecycle duties.

### **Structural Amendments**
Modify adjacency rules, isolation boundaries, or subsystem/traversal relationships.

### **Governance Amendments**
Modify authority powers, override rules, or constitutional processes.

### **Stability Amendments**
Modify drift‑neutrality rules, lifecycle phases, or stability guarantees.
