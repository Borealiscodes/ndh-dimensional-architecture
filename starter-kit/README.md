# Starter Kit  
### A Gentle, Bob-Ross-Style Beginning for Your Project

## 1. Purpose
Welcome to your Starter Kit.  
This folder gives you a calm, friendly foundation for building any project, system, or architecture.  
Everything here is designed to be simple, gentle, and non-coercive — a happy little place to begin.

This kit does not tell you what to do.  
It simply offers tools, templates, and soft guidance so you can build at your own pace.

---

## 2. What’s Inside
The Starter Kit contains several helpful areas:

- **core/** — foundational identity documents  
- **workflow/** — templates for designing your personal or team workflow  
- **meta/** — high-level maps and indexes  
- **docs/** — descriptive documents and style guides  
- **boundaries/** — edges, constraints, and release guidelines  
- **operations/** — how your project behaves or functions  
- **stability/** — coherence and consistency documents  
- **appendices/** — optional extended materials  
- **templates/** — reusable templates for new documents  

Each folder is small, friendly, and expandable.

---

## 3. How to Use This Starter Kit
You can use this kit in any order, but here’s a gentle workflow:

### Step 1 — Pick One Folder  
Choose whichever area feels light today.  
Maybe **core**, maybe **workflow**, maybe **meta**.

### Step 2 — Find a Tiny Gap  
Look for one small thing you want to add or clarify.  
A definition, a boundary, a relationship, a rule.

### Step 3 — Add One Gentle Stroke  
Write one small, clear line.  
Not a whole tree — just a happy little branch.

### Step 4 — Step Back  
Pause and look at your change.  
If it feels stable, you’re done.  
If it feels wobbly, add one more tiny stroke.

### Step 5 — Stop Early  
Leave room for future joy.  
Projects grow best when you don’t overfill the canvas.

### Step 6 — Commit the Change  
Save the file.  
Push the update.  
Let the canvas dry.

---

## 4. What This Kit Is Not
This kit is not:
- a rulebook  
- a demand  
- a rigid structure  
- a governance system  
- a set of obligations  

It is a palette.  
You choose the colors.  
You choose the strokes.

---

## 5. Expanding the Starter Kit
As your project grows, you can add:

- new folders  
- new templates  
- new workflows  
- new boundaries  
- new maps  

The Starter Kit is designed to grow with you.
