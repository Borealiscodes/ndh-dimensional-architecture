- ND‑paced  
- reversible  
- symbolic

- continuity‑safe  

You can move slowly.

## 🌿 2. What You Will Be Creating  
As an adventure contributor, you will work with three core building blocks:

- **World Nodes** — places  
- **Character Nodes** — someone  
- **Story Nodes** — moments  

These combine into the **multi‑mode adventure loop**:

```
World → Character → Story → World → Character → Story → …
```

This loop is reversible and gentle.

## 🌳 3. The Adventure Architecture

### **Character‑Creator Mode**

- tone  
- ability

- safety header  

### **Story‑Weaver Mode**  
Creates reversible narrative moments using:

- soft quests  
- reversible scenes  
- pacing membranes  
- gentle arcs  
- stillness checkpoints

## 🔮 5. Writing Story Fragments  
Contributors can begin by writing **story fragments**.

Fragments can be:

- world details  
- character traits  
- soft quests  
- moods  
- symbolic echoes  

Examples:

```
"a soft hum behind a tree"
"a wanderer who hesitates before stepping forward"
"a pool that reflects sound instead of light"
```
