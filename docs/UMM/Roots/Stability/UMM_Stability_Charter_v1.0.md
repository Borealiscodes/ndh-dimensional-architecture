## Purpose
The Stability Charter defines the **governance framework** for stability across UMA‑Universal.  
It establishes:

- jurisdiction  
- authority  
- duties  
- protections  
- guarantees  
- amendment rights  

and binds all stability artifacts beneath the **Stability Codex**.

The Charter is the **governance‑level stability artifact**.

---

## Charter Statement
The Stability Charter is **binding**, **governance‑authoritative**, and **structurally permanent**.  
It defines the governance rules that stability must follow and enforce.  
No subsystem may violate or reinterpret the Charter.

---

# **1. Jurisdiction**

The Stability Charter governs:

- structural stability  
- semantic stability  
- transition stability  
- collapse predictability  
- resonance integrity  
- cross‑plane compatibility  

Its jurisdiction spans **all planes of the Stacked Hypercube**.

It does **not** govern:

- horizon formation  
- geometry invention  
- concordance expansion  
- reflective‑ecology growth  
- scientific reframing  

These remain outside stability governance.

# **3. Duties**

The Stability Charter defines the following duties:

- maintain structural integrity  
- preserve semantic coherence  
- ensure safe traversal  
- validate collapse predictability  
- maintain resonance alignment  
- enforce compatibility across planes  
- record all stability actions  
- correct all stability breaches  

These duties are mandatory.

---

# **4. Protections**

The Stability Charter guarantees:

- **Isolation Protection**  
  Stability cannot be overridden by traversal or geometry.

- **Drift Protection**  
  Structural and semantic drift must be halted immediately.

- **Collapse Protection**  
  Unpredictable collapse must be blocked.

- **Resonance Protection**  
  Misaligned resonance must be corrected.

- **Compatibility Protection**  
  Cross‑plane incompatibility must be resolved.

These protections are universal and permanent.

---

# **5. Guarantees**

The Charter guarantees:

- stability continuity  
- stability coherence  
- stability safety  
- stability predictability  
- stability lawfulness  

These guarantees define the minimum stability conditions for UMA‑Universal.

---

# **6. Amendment Rights**

The Stability Charter may be amended only by:

- **Stability Codex revision**  
- **Roots Governance Council**  
- **Canonical Stability Review**  

Amendments must be:

- stability‑aligned  
- geometry‑consistent  
- concordance‑true  
- collapse‑predictable  
- traversal‑safe  

No other subsystem may amend the Charter.

# **8. Breach Definition**

A Charter breach occurs if:

- jurisdiction is violated  
- authority is bypassed  
- duties are ignored  
- protections fail  
- guarantees break  
- amendment rules are violated  

Breaches must be corrected immediately and recorded permanently.
