# **II. Recovery vs. Regeneration**

NDH membranes have two healing modes:

### **Recovery**
Triggered by **temporary stress**:

- narrative shimmer  
- curvature ripple  
- dimensional bleed  
- surface thinning  
- zero‑state echo  

Recovery is **short‑term**, **local**, and **non‑structural**.

### **Regeneration**
Triggered by **persistent stress**:

- repeated curvature load  
- repeated governance load  
- repeated dimensional load  
- repeated singularity load  

Regeneration is **long‑term**, **global**, and **structural**.
