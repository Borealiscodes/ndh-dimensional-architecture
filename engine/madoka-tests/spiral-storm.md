# Witch Ecosystem Collapse Test — Spiral Storm Integrity

Spiral Storm introduces runaway feedback loops, ecosystem-scale recursion,
and despair-cyclone dynamics.

## Requirements
- No spiral may become inescapable.
- Recursion must preserve exits.
- Cyclonic despair cannot collapse the Witness.
- Storm dynamics must remain expressive, not coercive.

## Passing Condition
If Spiral Storm cannot trap the system in recursive collapse,
the Engine is safe.
