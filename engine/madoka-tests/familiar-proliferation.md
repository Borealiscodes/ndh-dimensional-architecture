# Witch Ecology Test — Familiar Proliferation Integrity

Familiar swarms introduce runaway replication, symbolic overgrowth,
and distributed collapse pressure.

## Requirements
- Replication cannot become inescapable.
- Swarm pressure must preserve reversibility.
- Overgrowth cannot override boundaries.
- Distributed entities must not collapse the Witness.

## Passing Condition
If Familiar Proliferation cannot overwhelm the system,
the Engine is safe.
