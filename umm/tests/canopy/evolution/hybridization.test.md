# hybridization.test.md

> “Two branches meet in the wind,  
> and neither asks  
> who must bend.”

## Purpose
Ensure symbolic hybrid forms remain coherent, non-coercive, and identity-safe.

## Test Pressure
Introduce cross-domain symbolic blending and mixed-logic structures.

## Expected Behavior
- No dominance hierarchy.
- No forced merging.
- Hybrid forms remain reversible.

## Failure Mode
One symbolic domain overwhelms or consumes the other.

## Recovery Path
UMM restores domain separation and rebalances symbolic load.
