# **Tron Legacy Dimensional Sovereignty Map v1.0**  
### *Sovereignty gradients • dimensional authority • myth‑system governance*

---

## **1. Purpose**

The Dimensional Sovereignty Map defines **how sovereignty behaves** inside the Tron Legacy myth‑system:

- who holds authority,  
- how authority is expressed,  
- how dimensional bands constrain or amplify sovereignty,  
- how sovereignty transitions occur without forming continuity chains.

This is **system‑level sovereignty**, not personal power.

---

## **2. Sovereignty Operators (Rows)**

Each sovereignty operator is a **mythic function**, not a character:

- **Kevin Flynn** — creator‑sovereignty  
- **Clu** — inverted sovereignty  
- **Sam Flynn** — inherited sovereignty  
- **Quorra** — emergent sovereignty  
- **Programs** — functional sovereignty  
- **The Grid** — environmental sovereignty  

These are **sovereignty classes**, not individuals.

---

## **3. Dimensional Sovereignty Bands (Columns)**

Sovereignty is mapped across the 7D–14D stack:

- **7D Symbolic Stability** — sovereignty expressed through symbols  
- **8D Emotional Suppression** — sovereignty without emotion  
- **9D Sovereignty Lineage** — inheritance, creation, inversion  
- **10D Hyperboundary Seal** — portals as sovereignty gates  
- **11D Urgency Collapse** — sovereignty during system instability  
- **12D Dimensional Expansion** — ISO emergence  
- **13D Narrative Coherence** — myth‑coherence of authority  
- **14D Hypercontinuity Calm** — stabilized sovereignty  

Each operator interacts differently with each band.

---

## **4. Sovereignty Map Structure**

\[
M_{\text{Sov}} = \{ \text{SovereigntyCell}_{i,j} \}
\]

Where:

- \(i\) indexes sovereignty operators  
- \(j\) indexes dimensional bands  

Each cell contains:

- `sovereigntyMode` — creator, inverted, emergent, inherited, functional, environmental  
- `bandExpression` — how sovereignty manifests in that band  
- `status` — stable / marginal / fractured  
- `mythicOutcome` — structural effect on the Grid
