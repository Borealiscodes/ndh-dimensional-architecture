# 🌱 **SPRING — Instructor Module: Emergence & Soft Beginnings**  
### *Season Theme: Gentle opening, curiosity, small motions.*

Spring is the season where instructors **introduce**, **invite**, and **open**.

## Instructor Priorities
- establish ND‑paced classroom norms  
- introduce the Compass  
- teach first maps  
- open first worlds  
- introduce first characters  
- keep everything tiny and reversible  

## Instructor Seasonal Compass
- **North — Structure**: keep sessions minimal  
- **South — Safety**: reduce sensory load  
- **East — Tone**: choose soft textures  
- **West — Cosmology**: keep cosmology outside  
- **Center — Pacing**: check group pacing often  

## Spring Teaching Flow
1. Stillness  
2. Compass check  
3. One map  
4. One world  
5. One character  
6. One Play Tree action  
7. One soft detail  
8. Close softly  

## Spring Instructor Notes
- avoid complex branching  
- avoid lore or cosmology  
- avoid multi‑plane work  
- keep everything symbolic and reversible  

---

# 🌿 **SUMMER — Instructor Module: Growth & Exploration**  
### *Season Theme: Warm expansion, gentle exploration, soft confidence.*

Summer is the season where instructors **expand**, **explore**, and **lengthen**.

## Instructor Priorities
- expand map regions  
- explore new world types  
- introduce second characters  
- lengthen drift paths  
- deepen Play Tree interaction  

## Summer Seasonal Compass
- **North**: expand structure gently  
- **East**: warm tone, gentle textures  
- **Center**: monitor pacing during expansion  

## Summer Teaching Flow
1. Stillness  
2. Compass tone check  
3. Expand one region  
4. Add one world motion  
5. Follow one extended branch  
6. Group pacing check  
7. Close softly  

## Summer Instructor Notes
- expansion must remain reversible  
- avoid rapid multi‑plane jumps  
- keep drift lines gentle  
- avoid emotional interpretation  

---

# 🍂 **AUTUMN — Instructor Module: Reflection & Integration**  
### *Season Theme: Gentle consolidation, soft harvesting, quiet clarity.*

Autumn is the season where instructors **simplify**, **review**, and **integrate**.

## Instructor Priorities
- review maps  
- simplify boundaries  
- consolidate worlds  
- observe character patterns  
- refine Play Tree pathways  

## Autumn Seasonal Compass
- **North**: simplify structure  
- **South**: stabilize the field  
- **Center**: slow pacing  

## Autumn Teaching Flow
1. Stillness  
2. Compass structure check  
3. Simplify one map  
4. Integrate one world  
5. Observe one character pattern  
6. Add one soft detail  
7. Zen Reset  

## Autumn Instructor Notes
- avoid adding new planes  
- avoid new characters  
- avoid new regions  
- focus on gentle consolidation  

---

# ❄️ **WINTER — Instructor Module: Rest & Deep Stillness**  
### *Season Theme: Soft rest, minimalism, gentle inwardness.*

Winter is the season where instructors **reduce**, **shrink**, and **restore**.

## Instructor Priorities
- reduce complexity  
- shrink the field  
- teach minimalism  
- emphasize pacing  
- restore the classroom  

## Winter Seasonal Compass
- **South**: safety through minimalism  
- **Center**: deep pacing checks  

## Winter Teaching Flow
1. Stillness  
2. Compass safety check  
3. Draw one simple symbol  
4. Pause  
5. Zen Reset  
6. Close softly  

## Winter Instructor Notes
- avoid expansion  
- avoid multi‑plane work  
- avoid new worlds or characters  
- keep everything tiny and quiet

- **Spring:** Map ↔ World  
- **Summer:** World ↔ Character  
- **Autumn:** Map ↔ Play Tree  
- **Winter:** Center ↔ Stillness  

These are soft pairings, not tasks.

Use: **Compass Center**
