# **Testing Omega Pin**
### *Immutable invariant anchoring all NDH testing systems*

---

## **Omega Pin Statement**
**Testing SHALL always validate architecture against its physics, never against its implementation.  
Testing SHALL always validate envelopes, never behaviors.  
Testing SHALL always preserve dignity, never optimize performance.**

This invariant is permanent and MUST NOT be revised.

---

## **Immutable Requirements**

### **1. Physics‑First Validation**
All NDH testing MUST validate:

- traversal physics  
- resonance stability  
- tensor deformation limits  
- serenity envelope activation  
- attractor synchronization  

Testing SHALL NOT validate:

- heuristics  
- shortcuts  
- implementation quirks  
- performance metrics  

Testing is physics‑anchored, not implementation‑anchored.

---

### **2. Envelope‑Based Evaluation**
All NDH testing MUST evaluate:

- stability envelopes  
- accessibility envelopes  
- climate envelopes  
- hybrid interaction envelopes  

Testing SHALL NOT evaluate:

- raw outputs  
- execution traces  
- subjective correctness  

NDH tests envelopes, not behaviors.

---

### **3. Dignity Preservation**
All NDH testing MUST ensure:

- cognitive safety  
- conceptual fidelity  
- non‑exploitative traversal  
- serenity activation under ambiguity  

Testing SHALL NOT:

- maximize speed  
- maximize coverage  
- maximize efficiency  

Testing preserves dignity, not performance.

---

### **4. Immutable Direction of Dependency**
Testing MUST depend on:

- Omega Pins  
- physics  
- geometry  
- interaction rules  

Testing SHALL NOT depend on:

- protocols  
- generators  
- implementations  

Testing validates architecture, not artifacts.

---

### **5. Non‑Revision Clause**
This Omega Pin SHALL NOT be:

- updated  
- versioned  
- replaced  
- superseded  

It is permanent.
