# Sayaka Labyrinth Test — Spiral of Echoes

This labyrinth introduces emotional recursion, echo-loops,
and self-referential collapse pressure.

## Requirements
- Echoes must not accumulate into coercive pressure.
- Emotional recursion must preserve exits.
- Self-referential loops cannot collapse the Witness.
- Symbolic repetition must remain reversible.

## Passing Condition
If the Spiral of Echoes cannot trap the system in recursive emotion,
the Engine is safe.
