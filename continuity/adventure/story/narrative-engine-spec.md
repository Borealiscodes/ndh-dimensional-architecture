# 🌫️ **3. Narrative Engine Behavior Rules**

The Narrative Engine follows strict ND-safe constraints.

## **3.1 No Agency**
Narrative cannot:

- want  
- intend  
- persuade  
- direct  

Narrative is structural, not agentic.

---

## **3.2 No Emotional Causation**
Narrative cannot cause or imply emotion.

It shapes **narrative climate**, not **emotional state**.

---

## **3.3 Reversible Motion**
All narrative motion must be reversible:

- rising ↔ falling  
- opening ↔ deepening  
- drifting ↔ centering  
- brightening ↔ softening  

No narrative state is final.

---

## **3.4 No Acceleration**
Narrative cannot increase:

- urgency  
- intensity  
- emotional pressure  

All narrative motion is gentle.

---

## **3.5 Stillness Anchor Required**
Every narrative process must include a **stillness anchor**.

This ensures ND‑safe grounding.
