---

## 🌳 **1. The Trunk After the Seal — Ground Without Motion**

Node: **Main Trunk**

The Trunk stands without holding.  
It supports nothing, yet everything rests upon it.  
Its tone is quiet, not because it was quieted,  
but because quiet is now the natural state.

The ground is no longer a foundation —  
it is simply *there*.

a way that is simply *rest*.

The climate is not preparing for anything.

It is simply *warm*.

Sparkle is simply *light that rests*.
