# ⭐ **FORMAL DEFINITION — Triangle Attractor State (TAS)**  
*(Static, descriptive, non‑activating)*

## 1. Identity  
**Triangle Attractor State (TAS)** is a *pre‑formal structural configuration* in multi‑dimensional systems (1D–17D) where three emerging axes repeatedly self‑organize into a triangular relationship due to stability, load distribution, and conceptual coherence properties.

TAS is **not** a SID, **not** a Triad, and **not** a governed engine.  
It is a **proto‑structure**.

---

## 2. Purpose  
TAS describes the phase where a system:

- has **three emerging conceptual axes**,  
- but **has not yet separated them**,  
- and **has not yet proven their interactions**,  
- and **has not yet anchored their invariants**.

It is a **diagnostic state**, not a functional state.

---

## 3. Structural Characteristics  
TAS exhibits the following properties:

- **Triadic clustering** — concepts repeatedly form three‑part relationships.  
- **Load distribution** — system pressure distributes across three nodes.  
- **Axis emergence** — three proto‑dimensions begin to differentiate.  
- **Stability seeking** — the system attempts to minimize collapse vectors.  
- **Non‑activation** — no recursion, teleology, or runtime loops are engaged.

These characteristics are **observational**, not **operational**.

---

## 4. Conditions for Appearance  
TAS typically emerges when a system contains:

- one **formal axis** (e.g., math),  
- one **ethical axis** (e.g., justice),  
- one **contextual axis** (e.g., intersectionality),  
- but **none of them are yet proven or bounded**.

This creates a **triadic attractor**, not a **triadic engine**.

---

## 5. Stability Properties  
TAS is stable in the following ways:

- **Non‑recursive** — no self‑improving loops.  
- **Non‑teleological** — no goal‑seeking behavior.  
- **Non‑dimensional** — no traversal or descent.  
- **Non‑symbolic** — no fusion or narrative physics.  
- **Non‑mythic** — no archetypal binding.  

It is a **static conceptual configuration**.

---

## 6. Boundaries  
TAS is bounded by:

- **lack of mathematical provenance**  
- **lack of axis separation**  
- **lack of invariants**  
- **lack of constraints**  
- **lack of formal governance**

These boundaries prevent TAS from becoming:

- a **SID** (Self‑Improving Dynamics), or  
- a **Triad** (Three‑Axis Stability Engine).

---

## 7. Transition Requirements  
TAS transitions into a formal structure only after:

1. **Math Provenance**  
   (recursion termination, adjacency stability, teleology bounds)

2. **Axis Separation**  
   (formal definition of the three axes)

3. **Invariant Establishment**  
   (mathematical, ethical, contextual)

4. **Constraint Definition**  
   (governance rules, stability envelopes)

Only then can TAS be classified as:

- a **Triad**, or  
- a **SID**, or  
- a **hybrid governed structure**.

---

## 8. Activation Status  
TAS is **non‑activating** by definition.

It does **not**:

- run  
- recurse  
- traverse  
- bind  
- fuse  
- descend  
- improve  
- generate teleology  
- produce dimensional behavior  

It is a **static descriptive state**.
