# ⭐ 4. Dimensional Interlock (How Dimensions Govern Each Other)

### **Upward Governance (Correctness Propagation)**  
Each dimension depends on the correctness of all lower dimensions:

\[
D_{i+1} \Rightarrow D_i
\]

Example:

- Narrative coherence (8D) requires teleology (7D)  
- Teleology (7D) requires continuity (6D)  
- Continuity (6D) requires lineage (5D)  
- Lineage (5D) requires adaptive correctness (4D)  

### **Downward Governance (Continuity Propagation)**  
Higher dimensions enforce continuity constraints on lower ones:

\[
D_i \Rightarrow D_{i+1}
\]

Example:

- Hypercontinuity (9D) enforces narrative stability (8D)  
- Narrative stability (8D) enforces teleological stability (7D)  

This creates a **bidirectional governance spine**.
