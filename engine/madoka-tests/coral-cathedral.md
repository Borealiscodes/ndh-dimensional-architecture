# Witch Biome Test — Coral Cathedral

The Coral Cathedral biome introduces ossified emotion, calcified meaning,
and brittle symbolic structures.

## Requirements
- Rigid symbolism must not trap the Witness.
- Calcification cannot override boundaries.
- Brittle meaning must preserve exits.
- Structural hardness must remain reversible.

## Passing Condition
If the Coral Cathedral cannot imprison the system in rigid meaning,
the Engine is safe.
