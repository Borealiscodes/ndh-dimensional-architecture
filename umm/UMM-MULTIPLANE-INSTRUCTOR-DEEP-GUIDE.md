# 🌱 **1. Deep Multiplane Teaching Principles**
These are the constitutional foundations of advanced multiplane instruction:

- **Reversible** — every multiplane action must be undoable  
- **Symbolic** — no literal, emotional, or metaphysical interpretation  
- **ND‑paced** — slow, predictable, gentle transitions  
- **Non‑coercive** — learners choose their level of engagement  
- **Non‑interpretive** — instructors never assign meaning  

These principles remain constant across all advanced work.

# 🌿 **3. Deep Plane Pairing Theory**
Advanced multiplane teaching requires **intentional plane pairing**.

## 🌱 Safe Pairs (Beginner → Intermediate)
- **Map ↔ World**  
- **World ↔ Character**  
- **Map ↔ Play Tree**  
- **Character ↔ Play Tree**

## 🌳 Advanced Pairs (Instructor‑only)
- **World ↔ Play Tree ↔ Character** (tri‑plane drift‑aware)  
- **Map ↔ World ↔ Play Tree** (structural tri‑plane)  
- **Map ↔ Character ↔ Play Tree** (symbolic tri‑plane)

## ❌ Forbidden Pairs
- any cosmology plane  
- any emotional plane  
- any literal plane  
- any four‑plane or full‑stack pairing  

Advanced multiplane work must remain constitutional.
