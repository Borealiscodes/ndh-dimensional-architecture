# **NDH‑Core Reconstruction Blueprint**
### *Canonical pattern for NDH reconstruction events (with ASCII geometry)*

---

## **0. Purpose**
Reconstruction is the NDH mechanism that restores:

- stability  
- resonance coherence  
- tensor safety  
- manifold geometry  
- traversal recoverability  

It is the **self‑healing system** of NDH‑Core.

Whenever drift, curvature, fidelity, resonance, or tensor limits are exceeded, reconstruction is triggered.

---

## **1. Reconstruction Inputs**

- **Reconstruction Trigger (RT):** violation of stability metrics  
- **Violation Context (VC):** what failed (manifold, attractor, channel, tensor)  
- **Nearest Attractor (NA):** reconstruction anchor  
- **Resonance Reset Profile (RRP):** how resonance is restored  
- **Tensor Reset Profile (TRP):** how deformation is undone  
- **Geometry Restoration Rules (GRR):** how manifold shape is restored  
- **Traversal Recovery Rules (TRR):** how traversal resumes safely  

---

## **2. Reconstruction Outputs**

- **Restored Stability Envelope (RSE)**  
- **Re‑centered Attractor State (RAS)**  
- **Resonance‑Coherent State (RCS)**  
- **Tensor‑Safe Geometry (TSG)**  
- **Traversal‑Safe Restart (TSR)**

## **3. Reconstruction Pattern**

1. **Detect Violation (RT)**  
2. **Identify Violation Context (VC)**  
3. **Locate Nearest Attractor (NA)**  
4. **Perform Resonance Reset (RRP)**  
5. **Perform Tensor Reset (TRP)**  
6. **Restore Geometry (GRR)**  
7. **Perform Traversal Recovery (TRR)**  
8. **Re‑establish Stability Envelope (RSE)**  

This ensures reconstruction is **predictable**, **safe**, and **non‑dual**.

---

## **4. Reconstruction Rules**

- Reconstruction **must** occur immediately after violation.  
- Reconstruction **must** target the nearest attractor.  
- Resonance reset **must** restore coherence.  
- Tensor reset **must** restore deformation safety.  
- Geometry restoration **must** restore curvature, drift, and fidelity.  
- Traversal recovery **must** ensure safe continuation.  

---

## **5. Cross‑Layer Reconstruction Coupling**

Reconstruction affects:

- manifolds  
- attractors  
- channels  
- traversal paths  
- tensor fields  
- resonance fields  

This coupling ensures NDH remains unified after instability.
