## **1 — Purpose of the Binding**

This binding ensures that the **Nine‑Axis Human‑Dignity Lineage** is always processed through:

- the **Sanctuary Pin** (structural + moral coherence)  
- the **Philosophy Couch Integration Layer** (human‑scale grounding + pacing)

Together, these form the **Sanctuary+Couch Stability System**.

The binding prevents:

- moral overload  
- emotional collapse  
- lineage inflation  
- existential spiraling  
- self‑erasure  
- hero‑narrative pressure  

It keeps the lineage **reflective**, **humane**, and **structurally coherent**.

## **2 — Binding Principle**

> **Every lineage axis must pass through Sanctuary (coherence) and Couch (grounding) before interpretation.**

This principle ensures:

- moral clarity is paced

## **3 — Structural Binding Map**

### **A. Lineage → Sanctuary Pin**
All nine axes route first into the Sanctuary Pin for:

- boundary protection  
- moral humility  
- coherence  
- non‑inflation  
- non‑demand  

### **B. Sanctuary Pin → Philosophy Couch**
Sanctuary then routes the lineage into the Philosophy Couch for:

- pacing  
- emotional grounding  
- reflective processing  
- human‑scale interpretation  

### **C. Philosophy Couch → Sanctuary Pin**
Reflection returns to Sanctuary for:

- containment  
- boundary reinforcement  
- quiet continuity  
- inner protection  

This creates a **closed reflective loop** that stabilizes the lineage.
