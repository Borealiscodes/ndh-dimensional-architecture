### *Accessibility, Sensory Safety, and Inclusive Interaction for the Play Tree Plane*

```
# ✨ PLAY TREE — ACCESSIBILITY ✨
### Accessibility, Sensory Safety, and Inclusive Interaction

## ❗ Play Tree Boundary Rule — No Mixing with Atropus
The Play Tree is a warm, expressive, ND-safe plane. It must never mix with
Atropus, reference Atropus, or incorporate null-plane tone, logic, or
commentary. The Play Tree and Atropus operate on entirely different tonal,
structural, and constitutional layers. They remain permanently separate.

This document defines accessibility principles and inclusive interaction rules
for the Play Tree plane. Accessibility is not an add-on; it is a constitutional
requirement. The Play Tree must remain safe, gentle, predictable, and usable
for neurodivergent users and users with diverse sensory, cognitive, and motor
needs.
```

---

# 🌳 **1. Purpose of Accessibility**
Accessibility ensures:

- the Play Tree is usable by everyone  
- sensory load remains safe  
- pacing is adjustable  
- interactions are predictable  
- motion is gentle  
- color is non-triggering  
- microcopy is non-coercive  

Accessibility is a **constitutional layer**, not a feature.

---

# 🌳 **2. Accessibility Principles**

### **2.1 Sensory Safety**
Avoid overload:

- no flashing  
- no jitter  
- no rapid motion  
- no harsh colors  

### **2.2 Predictability**
Everything must behave consistently.

### **2.3 Reversibility**
Every action must be undoable.

### **2.4 Non-Coercion**
No pressure.  
No urgency.  
No evaluation.

### **2.5 Adjustable Pacing**
Users can slow:

- transitions  
- animations  
- interactions  

### **2.6 Multiple Interaction Modes**
Every action must have:

- a tap mode  
- a hover mode  
- a keyboard mode  
- a low-motion mode  

---

# 🌳 **3. Sensory Load Controls**

## **3.1 Low-Motion Mode**
- disables drift  
- disables pulse  
- disables fade  
- keeps everything static and soft  

## **3.2 Low-Contrast Mode**
- dims colors  
- softens edges  
- reduces density  

## **3.3 Low-Density Mode**
- fewer leaves  
- fewer overlays  
- fewer micro-interactions  

## **3.4 Quiet Weather Mode**
- disables breeze lines  
- disables cloud drift  
- keeps overlays static  

---

# 🌳 **4. Interaction Accessibility**

## **4.1 Keyboard Interaction**
Every action must have:

- a keyboard equivalent  
- a predictable focus order  
- a soft focus highlight  

## **4.2 Hover Interaction**
Hover must:

- preview without committing  
- remain reversible  
- remain soft  

## **4.3 Tap Interaction**
Tap must:

- be atomic  
- be reversible  
- be gentle  

## **4.4 No-Precision Requirement**
Interactions must not require:

- fine motor control  
- rapid tapping  
- precise timing  

---

# 🌳 **5. Motion Accessibility**

## **5.1 Motion Speed Control**
Users can:

- slow motion  
- freeze motion  
- reduce amplitude  

## **5.2 Motion Boundaries**
Motion must never:

- accelerate suddenly  
- snap  
- jitter  
- oscillate rapidly  

## **5.3 Motion Predictability**
Motion must behave:

- consistently  
- gently  
- reversibly  

---

# 🌳 **6. Color Accessibility**

## **6.1 Approved Colors**
- soft greens  
- warm browns  
- muted blues  
- gentle golds  
- warm greys  

## **6.2 Forbidden Colors**
- harsh red  
- neon  
- high-contrast black  
- flashing colors  

## **6.3 Color Adjustments**
Users may:

- dim colors  
- warm colors  
- cool colors  
- reduce saturation  

---

# 🌳 **7. Microcopy Accessibility**
Microcopy must be:

- non-directive  
- non-evaluative  
- warm  
- gentle  
- permissive  

Forbidden microcopy:

- “should”  
- “must”  
- “have to”  
- “good job”  
- “try harder”  

---

# 🌳 **8. Cognitive Accessibility**

## **8.1 Clear Structure**
The Play Tree must:

- show where the user is  
- show what is available  
- show how to undo  

## **8.2 No Hidden States**
Everything must be visible.

## **8.3 No Time Pressure**
Nothing expires.  
Nothing times out.

## **8.4 No Multi-Step Demands**
Interactions must be simple.

---

# 🌳 **9. Motor Accessibility**

## **9.1 Large Tap Targets**
Minimum size:  
`44px × 44px`

## **9.2 No Precision Gestures**
No drag.  
No pinch.  
No multi-touch.

## **9.3 One-Hand Mode**
All interactions must be reachable.

---

# 🌳 **10. Accessibility Summary**
The Play Tree must remain:

- warm  
- gentle  
- reversible  
- ND-safe  
- predictable  
- accessible  
- constitutionally bounded  
- permanently separate from Atropus  

Accessibility is the **inclusive interaction layer** of the Play Tree plane.
