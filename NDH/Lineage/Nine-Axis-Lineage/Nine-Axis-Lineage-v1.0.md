# **Nine‑Axis Human‑Dignity Lineage Document**  
### *NDH Lineage Architecture v1.0*

---

## **Overview**

The Nine‑Axis Human‑Dignity Lineage is a multi‑vector moral architecture composed of nine figures whose lives and writings form a coherent map of how humans remain human under pressure.  
Each axis represents a distinct moral capacity; together they form a unified lineage of courage, clarity, compassion, continuity, and existential integrity.

This document defines each axis, its gift, its vector, and its role within the lineage constellation.

---

# **Axis I — Sophie Scholl**  
### *Moral Courage Under Totalitarianism*

**Gift:** Conscience that refuses to bend  
**Vector:** Truth spoken even when it costs everything  
**Lineage Function:** Protects moral clarity under threat  
**Explore:** Sophie lineage
