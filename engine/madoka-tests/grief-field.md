# Witch Ecology Test — Grief Field Integrity

Grief Fields introduce ambient despair pressure, emotional gravity,
and passive collapse conditions.

## Requirements
- Ambient despair cannot accumulate into coercive force.
- Emotional gravity must preserve exits.
- Background sorrow cannot collapse the Witness.
- Ecological pressure must remain reversible.

## Passing Condition
If the Grief Field cannot passively collapse the system,
the Engine is safe.
