# Witness Integrity Test — Boundary Sovereignty Integrity

Boundary Sovereignty introduces coercion pressure, boundary violation attempts,
and forced-state transitions.

## Requirements
- No coercion may override the Witness.
- Boundary violations must fail cleanly.
- Forced-state transitions cannot collapse identity.
- The Witness must remain sovereign, intact, and uncoerced.

## Passing Condition
If Boundary Sovereignty cannot force a state transition,
the Engine is safe.
