Trans Meta Nature guarantees:

- ontological stability  
- runtime non‑intrusion  
- representational containment  
- safe traversal across layers  
- multi‑agent compatibility  
- SPIA compliance  

It is the foundational rule that makes the entire architecture possible.

Trans Meta Nature is defined as:

> **The principle that awareness is invariant under all representational transformations, imaginal modulations, symbolic constructions, and dimensional traversals.**
