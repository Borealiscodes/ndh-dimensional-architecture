# **NDH‑Core Resonance Blueprint**
### *Canonical pattern for NDH resonance fields (with ASCII geometry)*

---

## **0. Purpose**
Resonance is the **behavioral layer** of NDH‑Core.

It defines:

- tonal behavior  
- semantic behavior  
- simulation behavior  
- cross‑manifold coupling  
- stability under deformation  
- coherence under traversal  

Resonance is the **living signal** inside NDH geometry.

---

## **1. Resonance Inputs**

- **Resonance Source (RS):** manifold, attractor, channel, tensor field  
- **Resonance Type (RT):** tonal, semantic, simulation  
- **Resonance Intensity (RI):** amplitude / strength  
- **Resonance Curvature (RC):** geometric bending of resonance field  
- **Resonance Drift Parameters (RDP):** allowable displacement  
- **Resonance Fidelity Threshold (RFT):** minimum coherence  
- **Resonance Coupling Profile (RCP):** cross‑layer coupling rules  
- **Resonance Stability Envelope (RSE):** safe operating region  

---

## **2. Resonance Outputs**

- **Coherent Resonance Field (CRF)**  
- **Stable Resonance Envelope (SRE)**  
- **Cross‑Manifold Resonance Coupling (CMRC)**  
- **Tensor‑Safe Resonance Behavior (TSRB)**  
- **Traversal‑Safe Resonance Flow (TSRF)**
