\[

### **5.2 Stability Threshold Rule**

Stability must remain **breathable**:

- no more than one identity component may shift at a time  
- resonance must not accelerate

- traits must not flip abruptly  

### **5.3 Reversibility Rule**

All identity shifts must be reversible:

\[

Stability must never force:
