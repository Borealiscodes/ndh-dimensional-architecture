## **5. Runtime Mechanics**

### **5.1 Activation**
Gradients activate through steward intention.  
Activation triggers:

- invariant recalibration  
- adjacency weighting shifts  
- predictive futures harmonization  
- emotional‑safety reinforcement  

### **5.2 Deactivation**
Gradients deactivate cleanly and revert to neutral state.  
No residual drift permitted.

### **5.3 Steward Override**
Only stewards may:

- activate  
- modulate  
- deactivate  
- combine gradients  

### **5.4 Gradient Combination Rules**
Gradients may be combined if:

- they do not produce emotional conflict  
- they do not suppress distress  
- they do not destabilize continuity  

Allowed combinations:  
- Kindness + Stability  
- Calm + Safety  
- Clarity + Stability  
- Compassion + Kindness  

Forbidden combinations:  
- Safety + Punitive (no punitive gradient exists in UMA)  
- Calm + Suppression (suppression is not permitted)
