# 📄 **Expanded Universe Engine — Boundary Definition**  
*(Repo‑ready document)*

## 1. Purpose  
This document defines the **operational boundaries** of the Expanded Universe Engine.  
Boundaries ensure the system remains:

- coherent  
- cognitively safe  
- stable  
- predictable  
- expandable  
- collaborative  

They prevent runaway emergence and clarify what the engine **does** and **does not** do.

---

## 2. Boundary Categories  
The engine’s boundaries fall into six structural domains:

- **Scope Boundaries**  
- **Safety Boundaries**  
- **Interaction Boundaries**  
- **Module Boundaries**  
- **Narrative Boundaries**  
- **Non‑Engine Boundaries**  

Each domain defines what the engine supports and what lies outside its operational space.

---

## 3. Scope Boundaries  
### **What the engine *does***  
- Simulates narrative logic  
- Generates faction behavior  
- Supports augmentation systems  
- Models cosmic interaction rules  
- Produces cultural norms  
- Enables stylized combat  
- Maintains economy/barter logic  
- Supports case‑study‑driven research  

### **What the engine *does not***  
- Implement real‑world physics  
- Model real‑world weapons  
- Simulate real‑world harm  
- Provide legal, medical, or institutional systems  
- Replace human decision‑making
