- how resonance **moves**

Spirals are the **motion primitives** of NDH phenomenology.

## **1. Spiral Geometry Inputs**

- **Spiral Origin (SO):** manifold, attractor, or channel  
- **Spiral Type (ST):** inward, outward, neutral, harmonic  
- **Spiral Curvature (SC):** geometric bending of the spiral path  
- **Spiral Intensity (SI):** amplitude / strength  
- **Spiral Drift Parameters (SDP):** allowable displacement  
- **Spiral Fidelity Threshold (SFT):** minimum coherence  
- **Spiral Resonance Profile (SRP):** tonal/semantic/simulation behavior  
- **Spiral Tensor Coupling (STC):** deformation rules  
- **Spiral Stability Envelope (SSE):** safe operating region

- **Cross‑Manifold Spiral Coupling (CMSC)**
