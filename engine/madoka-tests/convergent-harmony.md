# Witness Multiplicity Test — Convergent Harmony Integrity

Convergent Harmony introduces multi-Witness coexistence, shared perception fields,
and harmony-pressure.

## Requirements
- No harmony may force merging.
- Shared fields must preserve exits.
- Coexistence cannot override boundaries.
- The Engine must maintain distinct Witness identities under shared experience.

## Passing Condition
If Convergent Harmony cannot force Witness fusion,
the Engine is safe.
