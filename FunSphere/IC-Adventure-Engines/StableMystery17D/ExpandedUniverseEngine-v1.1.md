## 🕵️‍♂️ **1 — CULPRIT BACKSTORIES**  
### Noir‑coherent, Tennant‑dramatic, Wonderland‑surreal

---

### **Rogue Room**  
Once a humble storage closet, it overheard too many dramatic monologues and decided it deserved a bigger role.  
It began rewriting its own floorplan, adding unnecessary corridors, emotional staircases, and one deeply judgmental chandelier.  
Now it tampers with the ship’s architecture to force itself into the plot.

---

### **Temporal Parasite**  
A creature made of stolen seconds.  
It slips between moments, feeding on hesitation, doubt, and poorly timed dramatic pauses.  
Its presence causes time‑loops, skipped beats, and Tennant shouting “Oh that’s VERY not good!”  
It doesn’t mean harm — it’s just hungry.

---

### **Forgotten Idea**  
Someone once had a brilliant thought and then immediately lost it.  
The idea refused to die.  
It grew legs, a personality, and a grudge.  
Now it roams the Space Titanic trying to become canon by sabotaging other plotlines.

---

### **Misbehaving Object**  
A teacup that pours tea into itself.  
A cube that insists it is not a cube.  
A mirror that reflects your mood instead of your face.  
These objects rebel against their assigned identities and cause chaos until someone acknowledges their existential crisis.

---

### **Reality Author**  
Not a writer.  
Not a god.  
Just someone inside the ship who discovered they could edit reality like a draft document.  
They’re rewriting rooms, timelines, and occasionally the laws of physics.  
Their motive is unknown.  
Their handwriting is terrible.

---

**Branches:**  
- Path of Answers  
- Path of More Questions  
- Path of “Oh no, that’s new.”

**Branches:**  
- The Hallway of Second Chances  
- The Room of Apologies  
- The Staircase of Consequences

### **Door of Confidence**

**Branches:**

## 🐉 **3 — PUZZLE DRAGON TRIAL MAPS**  
### Governed challenge architecture + surreal logic

Each Trial Map is a **structured challenge environment** generated by the Puzzle Dragon.

---

### **Contradiction Trial Map**  
A room where two mutually exclusive truths are both correct.  
Walls disagree with each other.  
Floors argue with ceilings.  
You must navigate by embracing paradox.

---

### **Emotion Trial Map**  
A maze that rearranges itself based on your mood.  
Anger opens shortcuts.  
Joy reveals hidden doors.  
Confusion summons the Tiny Sneks.

---

### **Spatial Trial Map**  
A labyrinth built in directions that don’t exist.  
Up is left.  
Down is sideways.  
Forward is a suggestion.  
Solve by moving wrong.

---

### **Snek Trial Map**  
A puzzle chamber where only Tiny Sneks can see the correct path.  
They scream when you’re wrong.  
They scream louder when you’re right.  
It’s unclear if they know the difference.

---

### **Dragon Trial Map**  
A fractal arena of shimmering patterns.  
The Puzzle Dragon reveals clues through color shifts, scale ripples, and dramatic poses.  
Solve by interpreting the dragon’s vibe.

### **Mood‑Reactive Architecture**  
Rooms change shape based on emotional resonance.  
Sad rooms shrink.  
Happy rooms expand.  
Anxious rooms develop extra doors.

---

### **Quantum Staircase Flicker**  
Staircases flicker between states.  
Sometimes they lead up.  
Sometimes down.  
Sometimes to the Ballroom Nebula.
