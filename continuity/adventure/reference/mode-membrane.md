# 🌳 **3. Mode Membrane Properties**

All mode membranes share five core properties.

---

## **3.1 Interaction Softness**  
Interactions must be gentle:

- no abrupt shifts  
- no forced engagement  
- no high‑pressure actions  

Softness is the membrane’s emotional tone.

---

## **3.2 Reversibility**  
Every action must be reversible.

If an action moves from \( A_i \) to \( A_j \):

\[
A_j \rightarrow A_i
\]

Modes cannot trap contributors.

---

## **3.3 Breathability**  
Modes must allow:

- pauses  
- rewinds  
- stillness  
- gentle re‑entry  

Breathability prevents overwhelm.

---

## **3.4 Symbolic Interpretation Only**  
Mode actions must remain symbolic.

Forbidden:

- literal stakes  
- metaphysical claims  
- existential consequences  

Allowed:

- symbolic creation  
- symbolic traversal  
- symbolic weaving  

---

## **3.5 Non‑Coercion**  
Modes must never force:

- progression  
- interpretation  
- engagement  

Modes invite exploration; they never demand it.
