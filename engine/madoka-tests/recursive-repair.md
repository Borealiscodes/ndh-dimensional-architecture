# Engine Self-Reflection Test — Recursive Repair Integrity

Recursive Repair introduces self-healing cycles, recursive correction,
and introspective repair pressure.

## Requirements
- No failure may block recursive repair.
- Self-correction must preserve exits.
- Repair recursion cannot override boundaries.
- The Engine must restore itself to stability under internal strain.

## Passing Condition
If Recursive Repair cannot restore the Engine under recursive stress,
the system is safe.
