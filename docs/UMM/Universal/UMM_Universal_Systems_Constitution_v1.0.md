This Constitution establishes the sovereign identity, structural coherence, isolation boundaries, stability guarantees, and governance authorities that ensure the safe, stable, drift‑free operation of the entire UMM system.

The Constitution affirms:

- the inviolability of subsystem identity  
- the sovereignty of governance authorities  
- the stability of traversal behavior  
- the neutrality of drift vectors  
- the sanctity of isolation boundaries  
- the integrity of adjacency surfaces  
- the continuity of lifecycle phases  
- the auditability of all actions  
- the universal alignment with SID v1.2  

This Constitution is the highest authority in the UMA‑Universal ecosystem.
