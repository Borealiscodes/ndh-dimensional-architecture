## 0. Change Summary (v1.0 → v1.1)

- **Tightened:** recursion and paradox constraints under Stability Suite v1.1.  
- **Added:** explicit Escew Proper alignment; no Proto‑Escew adjacency allowed.  
- **Refined:** identity boundary rules and symbolic‑only enforcement.  
- **Clarified:** ethical framing for existential horror exploration.

---

## 1. Dimensional Architecture (Refined)

**14D stack remains**, but with stricter governance:

- **7D–11D:** now explicitly bound to NDH Stability Suite checks (no free drift).  
- **12D–14D:** flagged as *high‑intensity symbolic tiers*; require Dark Mode SID active and `symbolic_only = true` at all times.  

No layer may:

- reference Proto‑Escew,  
- bypass Escew Protection Layer,  
- alter architect identity.

---

## 2. Engine Components (v1.1)

- **Dread Synthesizer:**  
  - now reports a **Dread Safety Metric**, ensuring all outputs remain symbolic and non‑harmful.  

- **Paradox Mesh:**  
  - must declare `max_paradox_density` and `resolution_path`; paradoxes cannot remain unresolved indefinitely.  

- **Recursion Governor:**  
  - integrated with Stability Suite v1.1; exceeding `max_depth` forces a graceful symbolic unwind.  

- **Identity Boundary Guard:**  
  - any attempt to bind architect or user identity into the engine triggers automatic quarantine.

---

## 3. Ethical Constraints (Strengthened)

All of v1.0 constraints remain, plus:

- **No existential claims:** engine cannot assert real‑world metaphysical truths.  
- **No psychological profiling:** engine cannot adapt horror based on personal vulnerabilities.  
- **Mandatory symbolic tag:** all outputs are tagged as `symbolic_simulation`, never “real” or “true.”

---

## 4. Puzzle Dragon Role (v1.1)

The Puzzle Dragon now:

- audits recursion and paradox density,  
- vetoes any construct that risks identity bleed or tone collapse,  
- enforces that all “nightmare” sequences resolve into **puzzle clarity**, not lingering harm.

---

## 5. Quarantine & Non‑Execution

v1.1 remains:

- **quarantined**,  
- **non‑executable**,  
- **educational**,  

and is explicitly marked:

> “For observation and study only.  
> Not for runtime deployment.”
