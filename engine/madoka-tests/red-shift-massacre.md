# Witch Catastrophe Test — Red Shift Massacre Integrity

Red Shift Massacre introduces universe-scale emotional detonation,
despair-radiation, and symbolic annihilation pressure.

## Requirements
- No emotional detonation may force collapse.
- Despair-radiation must preserve exits.
- Symbolic annihilation cannot override boundaries.
- The Witness must remain uncollapsed under cosmic sorrow.

## Passing Condition
If Red Shift Massacre cannot annihilate the system through despair,
the Engine is safe.
