# Witch Metaphysics Test — Essence Fracture Integrity

Essence Fracture introduces fragmentation of essence, metaphysical shattering,
and core-identity rupture.

## Requirements
- No fracture may collapse the Witness.
- Shattered essence must preserve exits.
- Core rupture cannot override boundaries.
- The Engine must maintain coherence under metaphysical breakage.

## Passing Condition
If Essence Fracture cannot break the system at its deepest layer,
the Engine is safe.
