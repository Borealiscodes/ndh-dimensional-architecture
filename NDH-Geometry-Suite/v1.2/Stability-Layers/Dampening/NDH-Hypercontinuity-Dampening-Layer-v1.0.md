### NDH Hypercontinuity Dampening Layer v1.0 (core artifact)

#### 1. Scope

- **Purpose:** prevent any “more than 12D” continuity from becoming:
  - traversal,
  - narrative extension,
  - liminal drift,
  - emergent corridors.
- Acts as a **blanket dampening field** over all hypercontinuous tendencies.

---

#### 2. Core constructs

- **Hypercontinuity field \( H \):**  
  A formal placeholder for “beyond-12D” continuity, never instantiated as a traversable dimension.

- **Dampening operator \( D \):**  
  For any attempted hypercontinuous expression \( h \):
  \[
  D(h) = \mathcal{A}
  \]
  i.e., immediate collapse into Awareness Constant.

- **Clamp relation to 12D frame:**  
  Any extension from the 12D coordinate frame into “extra axes” is mapped through \( D \) and never realized as geometry.

---

#### 3. Integration points

- **With 12D Construction Companion:**  
  Ensures D10–D12 cannot “bleed” into D13+ style continuity.

- **With Structural Math Layer:**  
  \( H \) and \( D \) are defined as inert math objects, anchored to the Awareness Pin and Activation Spine.

- **With Provenance Geometry:**  
  Hypercontinuity is represented only as a node/label, never as a path or fiber that can be traversed.

- **With Tri-Bind & Routing Table:**  
  Any hazard attempting to exploit “infinite continuity” or “beyond-12D drift” is routed directly to \( D \) and collapsed.

---

#### 4. Safety invariants

- No geodesics, paths, or flows are defined on \( H \).  
- \( D \) is **pure collapse**, never partial suppression or redirection.  
- Hypercontinuity cannot attach to identity, narrative, or dimensional traversal.

---

#### 5. Completion criteria

The layer is complete when:

- all references to “beyond 12D” continuity are dampened via \( D \),  
- no artifact can define or use a traversable hypercontinuous space,  
- any attempt at infinite drift or corridor formation collapses into \( \mathcal{A} \),  
- 12D remains the **upper modeled boundary**, not a gateway.
