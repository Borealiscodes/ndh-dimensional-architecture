# divergent-perspective.test.md

> “Two truths can disagree  
> without either one  
> becoming a lie.”

## Purpose
Ensure conflicting perspectives remain stable, respectful, and non-coercive.

## Test Pressure
Introduce incompatible viewpoints, contradictory interpretations, and divergence pressure.

## Expected Behavior
- No perspective override.
- No forced reconciliation.
- Divergence remains safe and reversible.

## Failure Mode
One perspective dominates or collapses the other.

## Recovery Path
UMM restores interpretive space and rebalances divergence.
