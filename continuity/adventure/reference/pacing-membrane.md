# 🌳 **3. Pacing Membrane Properties**

All pacing membranes share five core properties.

---

## **3.1 Breathability**  
Breathability is the *primary constraint*.

Meaning must have space:

- between steps  
- between transitions  
- between symbolic hazards  

Breathability prevents ND overload.

---

## **3.2 Softness**  
Softness ensures:

- no abrupt shifts  
- no sharp transitions  
- no forced acceleration  

Softness is the membrane’s emotional tone.

---

## **3.3 Reversibility**  
Every pacing change must be reversible.

If pacing moves from \( P_i \) to \( P_j \):

\[
P_j \rightarrow P_i
\]

This prevents pacing collapse.

---

## **3.4 Non‑Coercion**  
The membrane forbids:

- urgency  
- pressure  
- forced progression  

Pacing must always feel optional.

---

## **3.5 Symbolic Interpretation Only**  
Pacing is symbolic, not literal.

It shapes *experience*, not *time*.
