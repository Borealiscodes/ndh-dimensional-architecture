Define the **stability grid** \( S \) as:

\[

\begin{cases}
1 & \text{if cell } v_{ij} \text{ is stable (inert)} \\

\end{cases}
\]

All NDH visuals must satisfy:

\[
\forall v_{ij},\ S_{ij} = 1
\]

This guarantees full inert stability.
