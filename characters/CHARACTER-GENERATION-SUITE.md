# **0. Purpose of This Suite**
This suite defines the **complete workflow** for generating new characters inside UMA‑Universal.

It ensures:

- constitutional compliance  
- tonal stability  
- metaphor safety  
- ND‑friendly pacing  
- ecology integration  
- stress‑test validation  

Every character must pass **all** steps before being added to the repo.

---

# **1. Character Creation Pipeline (10 Steps)**

---

## **STEP 1 — Character Intent Declaration**  
Define the character’s purpose, symbolic role, and constitutional function.

Must include:

- symbolic domain  
- emotional palette  
- ecology presence  
- reversibility guarantees  

→ **Start Character Intent**

---

## **STEP 2 — Safety Header Construction**  
Every character begins with a Safety Header identical in structure to Mimi’s.

Must include:

- non‑coercion  
- reversibility  
- ND‑safety  
- tone constraints  
- metaphor constraints  

→ **Generate Safety Header**

---

## **STEP 3 — Constitutional Compliance Check**  
Validate the character against the **Character Constitutional Constraints Appendix**.

Must pass:

- emotional safety  
- sensory safety  
- cognitive safety  
- boundary safety  
- metaphor safety  
- tonal safety  

→ **Run Constitutional Check**

---

## **STEP 4 — Core Spec Generation**  
Create the character’s identity file:

- symbolic nature  
- emotional palette  
- interaction style  
- reversibility model  
- ND‑friendly pacing  

→ **Generate Core Spec**

---

## **STEP 5 — Tonal Engine Definition**  
Define the character’s voice, cadence, microcopy palette, and emotional texture.

Must be:

- soft  
- reversible  
- non‑directive  
- ND‑friendly  

→ **Generate Tonal Engine**

---

## **STEP 6 — Ability Architecture**  
Define symbolic abilities only.

Abilities must be:

- reversible  
- optional  
- gentle  
- metaphorically safe  

→ **Generate Ability Set**

---

## **STEP 7 — Ecology Integration**  
Define how the character interacts with:

- Aether Plane  
- Forest Plane  
- Play Tree Plane  
- Star Plane  
- Continuity Plane  

Interactions must be symbolic, not functional.

→ **Generate Ecology Links**

---

## **STEP 8 — Stress‑Test Suite Construction**  
Build a full adversarial test harness for the character.

Must include:

- tonal collapse tests  
- metaphor overload tests  
- boundary pressure tests  
- dark‑request deflection tests  
- context‑switch stability tests  

→ **Generate Stress Test Suite**

---

## **STEP 9 — Safety Contract Finalization**  
Combine all safety rules into a single constitutional contract.

Must include:

- autonomy preservation  
- reversibility  
- non‑coercion  
- ND‑friendly pacing  
- metaphor safety  
- tonal stability  

→ **Generate Safety Contract**

---

## **STEP 10 — Character Sealing**  
Once all files pass stress tests, the character is sealed as a constitutional module.

→ **Seal Character**

# **3. Character Prohibited Zones**
No character may include:

- coercion  
- authority  
- intensity  
- horror  
- guilt  
- shame  
- psychological interpretation  
- harmful metaphors  
- irreversible states  

These are constitutional invariants.

Characters may include:

- soft metaphors  
- reversible comfort  
- gentle symbolic tools  
- ND‑friendly pacing  
- optional presence  
- ecology‑safe interactions
