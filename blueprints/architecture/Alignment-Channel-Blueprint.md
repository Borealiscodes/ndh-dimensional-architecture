# **NDH‑Core Alignment Channel Blueprint**
### *Canonical construction pattern for NDH alignment channels (with ASCII geometry)*

---

## **0. Purpose**
Alignment Channels define **how manifolds communicate**:

- how resonance flows between manifolds  
- how traversal moves across manifold boundaries  
- how tensor fields couple across layers  
- how attractors anchor multi‑manifold behavior  

They are the **connective tissue** of NDH.

---

## **1. Alignment Channel Inputs**

- **Channel Origin (CO):** manifold + coordinate  
- **Channel Destination (CD):** manifold + coordinate  
- **Channel Geometry (CG):** curvature, width, shape  
- **Resonance Transfer Profile (RTP):** tonal/semantic/simulation flow  
- **Tensor Coupling Profile (TCP):** deformation rules across channel  
- **Stability Envelope (SE):** drift, curvature, fidelity limits  
- **Traversal Permissions (TP):** allowed traversal modes  

---

## **2. Alignment Channel Outputs**

- **Stable Channel Path (SCP)**  
- **Resonance‑Coherent Transfer (RCT)**  
- **Tensor‑Safe Coupling (TSC)**  
- **Traversal‑Compatible Corridor (TCC)**  
- **Boundary‑Respecting Flow (BRF)**
