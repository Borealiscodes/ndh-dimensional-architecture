# 🌑 **1. Mission Profile**
Atropus is a **Null‑Expansion Plane**.  
In field terms, this means:

- nothing grows  
- nothing accumulates  
- nothing drifts  
- nothing branches  
- nothing textures  

It is the **zero‑movement zone** of the UMM ecosystem.

Use Atropus when you need:

- structural freeze  
- drift prevention  
- collapse stabilization  
- safe containment  
- Winter‑aligned stillness

# 🪐 **3. Entering the Null‑Plane**

**Entry Rule:**  
> Enter only from **Winter** or **Reset**.

Guided links:  
- **Winter System**  
- **Reset Engine**
